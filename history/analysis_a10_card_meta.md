## Upgrade Priority (derived from upgrade rates)

Cards that are almost always upgraded when taken — if you have Smith charges, these are tier-1 targets:

1. **True Grit** (96%) — take it, upgrade it immediately
2. **Pommel Strike** (92%) — prioritize over Strike at smiths
3. **Armaments** (92%) — upgrade-first card
4. **Uppercut** (91%) — very high priority
5. **Vicious** (85%) — upgrade for the draw-2 chain to work
6. **Anger** (73%) — upgrade if taken
7. **Thunderclap** (60%), **Expect A Fight** (59%), **Taunt** (58%), **Feel No Pain** (57%) — mid-priority

Low-priority upgrades: Shrug It Off (18%), Colossus (18%), Defend (19%), Offering (15%) — skip at smiths.

## Deck Size

- **Median: 31 cards** (mean 32.8, range 17–71)

- Starter card retention: ~3 Strikes, ~3.6 Defends, ~0.9 Bash remain in final deck
- **Skip aggressively**: boss card rewards skipped 53% of the time; monster rewards skipped 31%; elite rewards skipped 28%

**Takeaway**: Target 25–35 cards. Skip rewards that don't fit the build — the 53% boss skip rate shows top players are very selective at the peak reward moments.
