## Post-Mortem Analysis

### Run Summary
- **Outcome**: Death at Floor 43 (Act 3 Elite — Soul Nexus)
- **Ascension**: 0
- **Final HP**: 0 / 85
- **Final Gold**: 207

### What Worked Well

1. **Vul-combo engine (Vicious+ / Bash+ / Squash / Tremble / Bully / Dismantle)**: Consistently killed elites in 3–5 turns. Vicious+ draw-2-per-application was the deck's engine — once two copies were in play, any Vul card snowballed into a full hand refill and massive burst damage.
2. **Candelabra T2 burst window**: The +2 energy T2 reliably enabled 8-energy turns that wiped enemies with Dismantle+/Bully chains. Always planned the highest-damage turn for T2.
3. **Mr. Struggles + Mercury Hourglass passive chip**: By T5–T6, these dealt 14 passive AoE per turn — effectively free damage that finished off low-HP enemies without spending cards.
4. **Red Skull + Rupture snowball**: Hemokinesis → Rupture → Str stacking reliably reached Str 7–9 in later fights, amplifying every subsequent attack without explicit Str buffs.
5. **Pyre+ (+2e/turn) + Pumpkin Candle (+1e/turn)**: Early energy investment created a 4-base-energy platform that made later fights much more fluid.
6. **Choices Paradox**: Combat-start card selection gave consistent flex — Molten Fist for Vul, Tremble/Squash for Vul stacking, Drum of Battle (unfortunately) for tempo.

### What Went Wrong

1. **Drum of Battle in deck with Impervious**: Drum of Battle exhausts the top card of the draw pile at the START of each turn after it's played. With Impervious in the draw pile, the probability of losing it was extremely high over 3+ turns. This directly caused the run-ending death at Soul Nexus T3.

2. **Low HP entering Floor 43 elite (38/85 = 44%)**: The HP chain that led here:
   - Floor 38 (Slimed Berserker): exited at 8 HP. Burning Blood → 14 HP.
   - Floor 39 (Crystal Sphere): no combat, no heal.
   - Floor 40 (Rest Site): healed ~19 HP → 33 HP.
   - Floor 41 (Chest): no change.
   - Floor 42 (Battleworn Dummy): no damage taken (dummy doesn't attack). Burning Blood → 38 HP.
   - **Root issue**: Only one rest site between Floor 38 exit (8 HP) and Floor 43 elite. Should have prioritized a second rest site or avoided the Dummy event until HP was higher.

3. **Battleworn Dummy 300HP choice**: Entered with 33 HP and chose the hardest difficulty. Even with the Vul engine, T3 draw was Howl from Beyond+ only — can't deal 142 HP in one card without the full chain. Should have chosen 75 HP for a guaranteed relic reward (a relic is always worth more than a failed 300 HP attempt).

4. **No recovery after Floor 38 near-death**: Exiting at 8 HP with no rest site immediately ahead was a pathing failure. Should have treated HP below 20% as an emergency — take the next rest site even at the cost of skipping an elite.

### Deck Assessment at Death

**Strengths:**
- Vul stacking: Tremble, Squash, Thunderclap, Bash+ — could reach 8–10 Vul in one cycle
- Burst damage: Bully, Dismantle+, Howl from Beyond+ — 3–4 kill windows by T2–T3
- Block: Impervious (30 Block exhaust), Colossus+ (8 Block + 50% Vul reduction), multiple Defends

- Drum of Battle: high-risk card in a deck that needed Impervious to survive multi-turn fights.
- Deck size was growing — more Cards Rewards were accepted than necessary, diluting key draws.

### Lessons for Next Run

1. **Never take Drum of Battle when Impervious (or any critical exhaust-once card) is in the draw pile.** Drum of Battle's per-turn exhaust is probabilistic instant-death for those cards.
2. **Pick the Battleworn Dummy tier that you can definitely clear.** A guaranteed relic from 75 HP >> nothing from a failed 300 HP attempt. Only attempt 300 HP if the kill is mathematically confirmed for all 3 turns.
3. **Treat HP below 20% as an emergency.** Immediately path toward a rest site. Do not enter elites below 60% HP.
4. **Soul Nexus counter:** Impervious + Defend = 35 Block minimum to survive T3 Soul Burn after Drain Life (43 effective). Enter at 60%+ HP. Colossus+ on Drain Life turn reduces T3 to ~21 effective (play it tactically).
5. **Vicious+×2 is the run's core engine.** Protect both copies — avoid exhausting them or putting them at risk of Drum of Battle removal.
6. **Track passive chip totals late-game.** Mr. Struggles + Mercury Hourglass combined for 14 AoE by T5. Factor this into kill timing to reduce total cards needed.
7. **Rest site at or above 80% max HP → upgrade priority, below 80% → heal.** AGENTS.md guidance; failed to follow this entering Act 3 elites.
