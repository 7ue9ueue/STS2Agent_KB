- Kaiser Crab: kill Rocket first by default. Rocket/Laser is more dangerous than Crusher; do not let Rocket survive deep into the fight with Strength/Rage unless lethal is already lined up.
- Kaiser Crab: exhaust aggressively enough to cycle Bloodletting and key attacks; the earlier boss line failed because it left Rocket alive at 92 HP on round 9 with no block answer.
- Kaiser Crab: preserve Energy Potion for a boss turn where it funds Bash plus attacks, Second Wind/draw continuation, Blood Wall plus damage, or a Rocket kill timing; Power Potion should be used early if it gives a scaling or defense power.
- Boss entry: full HP after Regal Pillow rest; no more pathing before Kaiser Crab.
- Decimillipede lesson: synchronized final kills matter more than early single-target burst; assign Vulnerable/potion damage to the segment that enables an immediate kill.
