# Run: Ironclad — Floor 7 (Loss)

**Date**: 2026-04-28  
**Character**: Ironclad  
**Ascension**: 0  
**Outcome**: Died to Terror Eel (Elite, Act 1, Floor 7)

## Key Decisions

- **Neow**: Took Silver Crucible (upgraded first 3 card rewards). Good pick.
- **Cards acquired**: Rampage, Pommel Strike, Shrug It Off, Molten Fist+, Not Yet+
- **Path**: Fought through regular combats to an elite (Terror Eel) at floor 7

## What Killed the Run

Entered Terror Eel at ~55 HP after taking chip damage from earlier fights. Triggered Screech at ≤70 HP (stun turn + 99 Vulnerable applied to player). Used the stun turn well (Bash → Molten Fist+ → Rampage combo). But ran low on HP by turn 7 (17 HP, Eel at 5 HP). Had no potions saved. With 0 energy remaining and Eel alive, the fatal 33-damage attack (×1.5 = ~49 incoming) could not be blocked.

## Lessons

- Save potions for elite fights, especially Terror Eel. A damage potion could have closed the 5 HP gap.
- Enter elites at 60+ HP. The permanent Vulnerable after Screech means every mistake past that point is amplified.
- The Bash → Molten Fist+ → Rampage combo is effective but front-loaded. If it doesn't kill or nearly kill the Eel before Screech, a fast follow-up kill turn is needed.
- Rampage scaled to 19 damage by late combat — very strong, but requires surviving long enough to use it.
