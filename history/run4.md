# Ironclad Run 2 - Floor 1

- Outcome: in progress, Act 1 floor 1, Soul Fysh boss.
- Neow: chose Phial Holster over Booming Conch and Scroll Boxes.
- Reasoning: Phial Holster gave immediate Energy Potion + Clarity Extract and a potion slot while preserving 99 gold; Booming Conch only helps elites, and Scroll Boxes costs all gold for a forced three-card bundle.
- Pathing: planned center start for two early combats, then branch toward shop/elite if rewards and HP support it.
- Current deck: starter Ironclad deck.
- Current relics: Burning Blood, Phial Holster.
- Current potions: Energy Potion, Clarity Extract.
- Harness note: Energy Potion may require a short wait/re-poll before the added energy and playable cards are reflected reliably.

## Floor 2 - Monster

- Encounter: Seapunk. KB says fixed cycle is Sea Kick 11, Spinning Kick 2x4, then Bubble Burp for block + Strength.
- Result: won at 72/80 after Burning Blood.
- Combat notes: accepted damage to push Bash/Strike damage before Bubble Burp; used Energy Potion during the kill attempt.
- Rewards: took Unrelenting over Fight Me! and Body Slam; claimed Colorless Potion and 18 gold.
- Reasoning: Unrelenting adds immediate front-loaded damage and a strong upgrade; Body Slam lacks support, and Fight Me! gives enemy Strength.
- Current resources: 117 gold; Colorless Potion and Clarity Extract.

## Floor 3 - Monster

- Encounter: two Corpse Slugs. KB says they use staggered Whip Slap / Glomp / Goop cycles and start with Ravenous.
- Result: won at 64/80 after Burning Blood.
- Combat notes: used Colorless Potion for Panic Button to cover 14 incoming, then killed one slug with Unrelenting/Bash/Strike sequencing. The survivor gained 4 Strength and was Stunned, then attacked for 7x2 after the stun.
- Rewards: took Pommel Strike over Armaments and Uppercut; claimed 11 gold.
- Reasoning: Pommel Strike's KB notes strong early performance and high upgrade priority; it adds efficient damage and draw without adding another 2-cost attack.
- Current resources: 128 gold; Clarity Extract.

## Floor 4 - Monster

- Encounter: Toadpoles. KB says weak encounter has front Toadpole starting with Spiken and the other using Whirl.
- Result: won at 55/80 after Burning Blood.
- Combat notes: killed the Whirl attacker first with Unrelenting into free Bash/Strike. The remaining Toadpole gained 2 Thorns; attacking through Thorns kept the fight short but cost HP.
- Rewards: took Bloodletting over Second Wind and Rampage; claimed 19 gold.
- Reasoning: Bloodletting's KB notes strong early performance and it supports the deck's 2-cost attacks; Burning Blood offsets part of the HP cost.
- Current resources: 147 gold; Clarity Extract.

## Floor 6 - Monster

- Encounter: Calcified Cultist + Seapunk. Cultists KB says Calcified opens with Incantation for 2 Ritual, then attacks every turn; Seapunk follows Sea Kick, Spinning Kick, Bubble Burp.
- Result: won at 46/80 after Burning Blood.
- Combat notes: used Bloodletting to play both Defends plus attacks into Cultist; killed Cultist before Ritual escalated. Used Clarity Extract to draw Bloodletting, then converted that into energy for the final Strike on Vulnerable Seapunk.
- Rewards: took Anger over Thunderclap and Second Wind; claimed Snecko Oil and 16 gold.
- Reasoning: Anger adds 0-cost damage for Bloodletting turns; Thunderclap overlapped with Bash/Molten Fist and Second Wind lacked support.
- Current resources: 46/80 HP, 40 gold, Snecko Oil.

## Floor 7 - Event: Punch Off

- Outcome: died on floor 7 against two Punch Constructs.
- Decision: chose I Can Take Them / Fight over Nab. The Punch Off event KB says Nab gives Injury plus a random relic, while Fight gives greater rewards; Punch Construct KB says each construct cycles Ready, Strong Punch 14, then Fast Punch 5x2 + Weak.
- Combat notes: used Snecko Oil immediately. Bloodletting energy and Snecko Oil cost changes were visible after a short re-poll, not necessarily on the immediate state. Killed the first construct on turn 3 by spending all attacks into it and accepted the second construct's Fast Punch. The remaining construct then blocked on Ready, followed with Strong Punch for 14 while the hand had no block.
- What killed the run: entered the fight at 46 HP but spent too much HP in the first two turns; once at 10 HP, the second construct's Ready block made a kill impossible before the 14-damage Strong Punch.
- Lessons: Punch Off's fight option is dangerous without reliable block or enough burst to kill one construct before Fast Punch. Artifact also makes Bash's first Vulnerable application fail unless Artifact is stripped first.
