## Lessons So Far

- Against Waterfall Giant, account for the post-lethal explosion damage from accumulated Steam Eruption; do not spend all HP racing the last phase.
- Fight Me! should be aimed at enemies that die soon or fights where Ironclad can exploit the Strength faster than the enemy.
- Phantasmal Gardener punishes greedy single-target openers; prioritize early AOE or block before trying to focus one down.
- Do not enter Waterfall Giant low unless the deck can burst hard immediately; long defensive play lets Steam Eruption outgrow the remaining HP buffer.
- Two-Tailed Rats punish leaving an attacker barely alive because Frail plus turn-2 summons can force a rest before the boss.
