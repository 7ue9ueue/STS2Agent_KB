## Post-Run Reflection

- What worked: the run followed the Smith-default rule with explicit HP math instead of resting by reflex. Whirlwind+, Pyre+, True Grit+, Scrawl+, Fiend Fire+, Unmovable+, Feel No Pain+, Uppercut+, and the final Blood Wall+ all had concrete jobs. Pathing also improved: skipping forced/optional elites at medium HP and taking shops/rests preserved the win while still finding enough power.
- What worked: draw/energy was the core, not an afterthought. Battle Trance, Shrug It Off, Bloodletting, Scrawl+, Pyre+, and Lantern made the deck able to assemble Break+/Dominate/Bully/Whirlwind+/Fiend Fire+ turns; the late block package then converted setup turns into survival through Unmovable+, Blood Wall, Feel No Pain+, Anchor, and Body Slam+.
- What worked: Music Box and Mummified Hand were treated as sequencing relics. Waiting to make Break+, Uppercut+, Bludgeon, or Bully the first Attack mattered more than simply playing the leftmost Attack.
- What did not work: Act 2 Kaiser Crab was too close. Rocket should have been focused earlier, and the run leaned on Fairy as a buffer instead of preventing the damage curve. The win does not mean the boss plan was clean.
- What did not work: several combat turns overvalued setup into incoming damage. The Axebot Pyre turn leaked 17 because Frail reduced the doubled block output, and Louse Progenitor cost too much HP because offense was chosen without a guaranteed lethal.
- What would change next time: evaluate enchants and potions by damage packets. Vigorous should have gone to Fiend Fire over Break+ once Whirlwind was unavailable, and Gigantification should be prioritized over Flex when it can phase-skip with Vulnerable or Music Box.
- What was newly important: Pantograph changes final campfire math; at 54/80 it made boss entry 79/80, so Smithing Blood Wall+ beat Resting. Test Subject Phase 2 repeats Multi-Claw every turn. Draw-pile previews can be unreliable after draw/shuffle effects, so only the live hand after each action should drive the next move.
- Harness lessons: use `choose_event_option`, `choose_rest_option`, `claim_treasure_relic`, `select_card_reward`, `select_card`, and `confirm_selection` in the right screens. Play one action at a time, wait briefly, and re-poll after generated cards, Music Box copies, Mummified Hand discounts, potion use, or overlays.
