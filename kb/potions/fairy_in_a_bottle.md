# Fairy in a Bottle

## Description

When your HP would be reduced to 0, this potion is discarded and you heal to 30% of your Max HP.

## Strategy Notes

- Strong low-HP pathing insurance; keep over speculative potions when a forced elite or boss remains and lethal risk is real.
