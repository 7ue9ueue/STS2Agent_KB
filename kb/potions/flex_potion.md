# Flex Potion

## Description

Gain 5 Strength. At the end of your turn, lose 5 Strength.

## Strategy Notes

- Strong boss/elite burst potion, especially with multi-hit attacks, Vulnerable, or Energy Potion turns; use when it kills minions, creates lethal, or skips a dangerous attack cycle.
