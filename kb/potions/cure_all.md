# Cure All

## Description

Gain 1 Energy. Draw 2 cards.

---
<!-- META-AGENT NOTES -->

## Strategy Notes

- Strong flexible turn fixer when both energy and card access matter; compare against Energy Potion when the hand already has enough cards, and against Swift Potion when energy is not the bottleneck.
