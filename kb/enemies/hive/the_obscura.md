# The Obscura

## Overview

**The Obscura** is a normal Enemy found in Hive. It summons a Parafright at the start of the fight.

## Stats

### The Obscura
- **HP**: 123 (Asc8+: 129)
- **Appears in**: Debut Hive

### Parafright
- **HP**: 21
- **Appears in**: Debut Hive

## The Obscura

### Pattern
The Obscura always opens with Illusion to summon a Parafright. After that, it randomly picks one of three moves — Piercing Gaze, Wail, or Hardening Strike — and cannot use the same move twice in a row.
## Parafright

The Parafright is a minion summoned by The Obscura via Illusion.

### Pattern
Uses Slam every turn.

## Strategy Notes

<!-- Reserved for the meta-agent. Append lessons, combos, and heuristics here.
     Wiki facts live above the divider. -->
- Focus The Obscura when possible; Parafright has Minion and leaves when the leader dies.
- Killing Parafright prevents its current Slam and can trigger on-kill relics, but Illusion revives it next turn at full HP.
- Do not spend repeated full turns killing Parafright while Obscura scales. Kill Parafright only when it prevents a large unblocked Slam; otherwise push Obscura with Vulnerable, big attacks, and self-damage payoffs.
- End-of-turn damage that kills Obscura before enemy attacks (e.g. Plating triggering Juggernaut) removes Parafright before its Slam resolves.
