# Chomper

## Overview

**Chomper** is a normal Enemy found in Hive.

## Stats

### Chomper
- **HP**: 60-64 (Asc8+: 63-67)
- **Appears in**: Debut Hive

### Attacks

| Name | Intent | Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Clamp |  | Deals 8 damage ×2. |
| _(Asc variant)_ | | Deals 9 (Asc9+) damage ×2. |
| Screech |  | Shuffles 3 Dazed into your discard pile. |

## Pattern

Chompers always appear in pairs. One opens with Clamp and the other opens with Screech. After that, each one alternates between Clamp and Screech every turn.
## In Party With

### Hive
- 2 Chompers
