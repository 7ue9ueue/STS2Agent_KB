# Living Fog

## Overview

**Living Fog** is a normal Enemy found in the Underdocks. It summons Gas Bombs that explode for damage and then die.

## Stats

### Living Fog
- **HP**: 80 (Asc8+: 82)
- **Appears in**: Debut Underdocks

### Gas Bomb
- **HP**: 7 (Asc8+: 8)
- **Appears in**: Debut Underdocks

## Living Fog

### Pattern
Always uses Advanced Gas first, then alternates between Bloat and Super Gas Blast.
## Gas Bomb

Gas Bombs are minions summoned by Living Fog via Bloat.

### Pattern
Uses Explode every turn, dealing damage and killing itself.
