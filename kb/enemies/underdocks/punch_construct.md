# Punch Construct

## Overview

**Punch Construct** is a normal Enemy found in the Underdocks and Glory.

## Stats

### Punch Construct
- **HP**: 55 (Asc8+: 60)
- **Appears in**: Debut Underdocks

### Attacks

| Name | Intent | Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| READY |  | Gains 10 Block. |
| Strong Punch |  | Deals 14 damage. |
| _(Asc variant)_ | | Deals 16 (Asc9+) damage. |
| Fast Punch |  | Deals 5 damage ×2. Applies 1 Weak. |
| _(Asc variant)_ | | Deals 6 (Asc9+) damage ×2. Applies 1 Weak. |

## Pattern

Cycles through its moves in a fixed order: READY, then Strong Punch, then Fast Punch, repeating.

## Strategy Notes

<!-- Reserved for the meta-agent. Append lessons, combos, and heuristics here.
     Wiki facts live above the divider. -->
- Pattern: READY (T1, gains 10 Block) → Strong Punch (14 dmg) → Fast Punch (5×2 + 1 Weak) → repeat. Kill before Fast Punch applies Weak.
- With Mr. Struggles + Mercury Hourglass passives, READY's 10 Block is eaten by 5 AoE at T2 start (T2=2+Hourglass=3=5 chip) — only 5 Block remains by T2 player phase.
- In multi-enemy fights: kill Punch first to prevent Weak application. At Str 4+, Spite double-hit (18 dmg) kills it at 15 HP before it reaches T3 Fast Punch.
- In pairs, leaving one construct alive after the other's Fast Punch can still be dangerous: its next Ready adds 10 Block before a 14-damage Strong Punch.
