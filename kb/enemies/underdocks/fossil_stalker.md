# Fossil Stalker

## Overview

**Fossil Stalker** is a normal Enemy found in the Underdocks.

## Stats

### Fossil Stalker
- **HP**: 51-53 (Asc8+: 54-56)
- **Appears in**: Debut Underdocks

### Attacks

| Name | Intent | Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Latch |  | Deals 12 damage. |
| _(Asc variant)_ | | Deals 14 (Asc9+) damage. |
| Tackle |  | Deals 9 damage. Applies 1 Frail. |
| _(Asc variant)_ | | Deals 11 (Asc9+) damage. Applies 1 Frail. |
| Lash |  | Deals 3 damage ×2. |
| _(Asc variant)_ | | Deals 4 (Asc9+) damage ×2. |

## Pattern

Always starts with Latch.

After any move, the next move is chosen randomly with equal probability:
- 33% chance of Latch
- 33% chance of Tackle
- 33% chance of Lash

No restriction on repeating the same move consecutively.
## Notes

- Fossil Stalker begins combat with 3 stacks of Suck, which grants it 3 Strength each time it deals unblocked damage with an attack. This means letting attacks through without Block will cause it to snowball quickly.
    - Suck will also trigger when Fossil Stalker deals unblocked attack damage to Osty, making it dangerous to rely on him to soak up damage.
- Tackle applies Frail, reducing your block gained by 25%, making it harder to defend against subsequent attacks.
- Its multi-hit Lash (3×2) is especially dangerous if it has accumulated Strength from Suck, as each hit benefits from the bonus.
## In Party With

Appears as a solo normal encounter.
