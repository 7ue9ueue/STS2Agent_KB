# Cultists

## Overview

The **Cultists** are a pair of normal Enemies found in the Underdocks. The encounter consists of a Calcified Cultist and a Damp Cultist. Both follow the same pattern: they buff on their first turn, then attack every turn after.

## Stats

### Calcified Cultist
- **HP**: 38-41 (Asc8+: 39-42)
- **Appears in**: Debut Underdocks

### Damp Cultist
- **HP**: 51-53 (Asc8+: 52-54)
- **Appears in**: Debut Underdocks

### Attacks

### Attacks

## Calcified Cultist

### Pattern
Always uses Incantation first, then Dark Strike every turn after.
## Damp Cultist

### Pattern
Always uses Incantation first, then Dark Strike every turn after.
## Notes

- The Cultists will not gain Strength on the turn that they use Incantation.
