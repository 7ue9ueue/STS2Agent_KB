# Gremlin Merc

## Overview

**Gremlin Merc** is a normal Enemy found in the Underdocks. It starts with Surprise and Thievery, stealing gold with each attack. When the Gremlin Merc is killed, it summons a Fat Gremlin and a Sneaky Gremlin.

## Gremlin Merc

### Pattern
Cycles through its moves in a fixed order:
1. Gimme
1. Double Smash
1. Hehe
1. Back to Gimme

When the Gremlin Merc dies, its Surprise power triggers, summoning a Sneaky Gremlin and a Fat Gremlin. The Fat Gremlin inherits the stolen gold via Heist.
## Fat Gremlin

The Fat Gremlin is a minion summoned when the Gremlin Merc dies. It carries any gold the Gremlin Merc stole.

### Pattern
Uses Spawned on its first turn (does nothing), then uses Flee every turn after, escaping with the stolen gold.
## Sneaky Gremlin

The Sneaky Gremlin is a minion summoned when the Gremlin Merc dies.

### Pattern
Uses Spawned on its first turn (does nothing), then uses Tackle every turn after.

- The Fat Gremlin will flee with any gold the Gremlin Merc stole during the fight. Kill it before it escapes to recover the gold.
