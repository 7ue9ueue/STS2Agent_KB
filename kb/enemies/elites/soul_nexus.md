# Soul Nexus

## Overview

**Soul Nexus** is an Elite Enemy found in Glory.

## Stats

### Soul Nexus
- **HP**: 234 (Asc8+: 254)
- **Appears in**: Debut Glory

### Attacks

| Name | Intent | Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Soul Burn |  | Deals 29 damage. |
| _(Asc variant)_ | | Deals 31 (Asc9+) damage. |
| Maelstrom |  | Deals 6 damage ×4. |
| _(Asc variant)_ | | Deals 7 (Asc9+) damage ×4. |
| Drain Life |  | Deals 18 damage. Applies 2 Vulnerable and 2 Weak. |
| _(Asc variant)_ | | Deals 19 (Asc9+) damage. Applies 2 Vulnerable and 2 Weak. |

## Pattern

Opens with Soul Burn, then randomly uses Soul Burn, Maelstrom, or Drain Life each turn. No move can be used two turns in a row. All three moves are equally weighted.
## Trivia

*Soul Nexus appears in Maul's card art, being bitten by snakes.

## Strategy Notes

<!-- Reserved for the meta-agent. Append lessons, combos, and heuristics here.
     Wiki facts live above the divider. -->
- **Opens with Soul Burn (29 dmg)** every fight. T1 must have enough Block or be ready to take the hit.
- **Drain Life is the most dangerous move**: 18 dmg + 2 Vul + 2 Weak applied to the player. Vul on you makes next 2 enemy attacks hit 50% harder; Weak makes your attacks 25% weaker for 2 turns. Colossus+ (halve damage from Vul enemies) is the counter — play it on the Drain Life turn to reduce T3 Soul Burn from 43 → ~21 effective.
- **No repeat rule**: T2+ can be Soul Burn, Maelstrom, or Drain Life, but never same move twice in a row. After Drain Life → T3 is NOT Drain Life.
- **Enter at 60%+ HP minimum**. At 38 HP, Soul Burn (T1) + Drain Life (T2) → T3 Vul-boosted Soul Burn is near-unwinnable without Impervious.
- **Drum of Battle danger**: If Impervious is in your draw pile, do NOT have Drum of Battle active — very likely to exhaust Impervious at the worst time.
- **Vul stacking is strong offensively**: No Artifact. Tremble/Squash/Bash+ push to 5+ Vul. With Vicious+ (draw 2 per Vul app), Dismantle (double-hit at Vul) and Bully (scales with Vul stacks) = kill window by T3-T4.
- **T3 Block requirement after Drain Life**: Soul Burn × 1.5 Vul-on-you = 29×1.5=43 effective. Need Block ≥ (43 − your HP) to survive. Impervious(30)+Defend(5)=35 Block is minimum safe combo at 9 HP.
