# Phrog Parasite

## Overview

**Phrog Parasite** is an Elite found in the Overgrowth.

## Stats

### Phrog Parasite
- **HP**: 61-64 (Asc8+: 66-68)
- **Appears in**: Debut Overgrowth

### Attacks

| Name | Intent | Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Infect |  | Shuffles 3 Infection into your discard pile. |
| Lash |  | Deals 4 damage ×4. |
| _(Asc variant)_ | | Deals 5 (Asc9+) damage ×4. |

## Pattern

Phrog Parasite alternates between its two moves:
1. Infect
1. Lash
1. Back to Infect, and so on.

When Phrog Parasite dies, Infested triggers and 4 Wrigglers are summoned. They will be stunned and do nothing on their first turn. The fight continues until all Wrigglers are defeated.
## Notes

Whether the Wrigglers remain stunned on the player's next turn depends on whether the Phrog dies on the player's turn or its own turn.
- If the Phrog is killed normally with an attack card, the Wrigglers will **not** be stunned on the player's next turn.
- If the Phrog is killed by damage from an Orb, then the Wrigglers will **not** be stunned on the player's next turn.
- If the Phrog dies to either Poison or Doom, then this counts as dying on its own turn, and so the Wrigglers **will** be stunned on the player's next turn.
## Useful Cards

- Frontloaded damage cards like Perfected Strike, Backstab, and Defile can be useful for taking down the Phrog Parasite before it can add too many copies of Infection to your deck, as well as for finishing off individual Wrigglers quickly.
- Deathbringer, Deadly Poison and other cards which apply Poison or Doom can give you an extra turn before the Wrigglers attack if the Phrog dies to their damage.
- Attacks which target all enemies like Dagger Spray, Bone Shards or Whirlwind can damage all of the Wrigglers at once.
- Cards which can exhaust or discard the Infections, such as Burning Pact or Hidden Daggers, can prevent them from damaging you.
## Trivia

- The Paper Phrog resembles the Phrog Parasite.

- When killed by an attack card: Wrigglers ARE stunned for the immediate enemy turn after Phrog dies, but NOT stunned on the player's subsequent turn — you get exactly one free enemy phase, then they attack.
- Use the stunned turn to hit all Wrigglers with AoE (Explosive Ampoule, Thunderclap) to reduce HP before they start attacking.
- Vicious + Thunderclap in T5 can finish the last Wriggler efficiently: Thunderclap applies Vulnerable → Vicious draws 1 → Dismantle+ double-hits for easy kill.
