# Elites

**[STUB]** Wiki page is marked incomplete — verify numbers at source before relying on them.

## Overview

quiet

Elites are highly powerful enemies that are found in Elite Encounters which are marked separately from normal monster encounters. Defeating an elite will give you a random Relic, 35-45 () Gold, and a Card reward. The same Elite can appear multiple times in an act, but all 3 different Elites in an Act have to be encountered first before an Elite repeats. The same Elite cannot be encountered twice in a row.



## Ascension Modifiers

- : Elites are around 60% more common on the map.
- : Enemies and Treasure Chests drop 25% less Gold.
- : All Enemies have more HP.
- : All Enemies deal more damage.
## Relic Interactions

- Black Star: Elites will drop an extra relic.
- White Star: Elites will drop an additional Rare card reward.
- Booming Conch: At the start of Elite combats, draw 2 additional cards.
- War Hammer: Whenever you kill an Elite, Upgrade 4 random cards.
- Sling of Courage: Start each Elite combat with 2 Strength.
- Sword of Stone: Transforms into Sword of Jade after defeating 5 Elites.
