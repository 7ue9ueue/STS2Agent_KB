# Terror Eel

## Overview

**Terror Eel** is an Elite Enemy found in the Underdocks.

## Stats

### Terror Eel
- **HP**: 140 (Asc8+: 150)
- **Appears in**: Debut Underdocks

### Attacks

| Name | Intent | Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Crash |  | Deals 16 damage. |
| _(Asc variant)_ | | Deals 18 (Asc9+) damage. |
| Thrash |  | Deals 3x3 damage. Gains 6 Vigor. |
| _(Asc variant)_ | | Deals 4x3 (Asc9+) damage. Gains 6 Vigor. |
| Stun |  | Stunned. Does nothing. |
| Terror |  | Applies 99 Vulnerable. |

## Pattern

The Terror Eel alternates between Crash and Thrash, starting with Crash.

When its HP drops to its Shriek threshold (70, or ), it becomes Stunned and then uses Terror before resuming the Crash/Thrash cycle.

- Terror applies 99 Vulnerable, which effectively makes the player take 50% more damage from attacks for the rest of the fight.
## Trivia

Terror Eel's name and Terror skill are a reference to the card Terror from Slay the Spire.

## Strategy Notes

- Kill within 2 turns after Screech. The free Stun turn is your burst window; turn 3+ with Vigor-stacked Thrash (×1.5 incoming from Terror) is nearly unsurvivable at normal HP.
