# Knights

## Overview

The **Knights** are an Elite encounter found in Glory, featuring three knights fighting together.

## Stats

### Flail Knight
- **HP**: 101 (Asc8+: 108)
- **Appears in**: Debut Glory

### Spectral Knight
- **HP**: 93 (Asc8+: 97)
- **Appears in**: Debut Glory

### Magi Knight
- **HP**: 82 (Asc8+: 89)
- **Appears in**: Debut Glory

## Flail Knight

### Pattern
Opens with Ram, then randomly uses Breaker, Flail, or Ram each turn. Breaker cannot be used two turns in a row.
## Spectral Knight

### Pattern
Opens with Hex. After that, alternates between Soul Slash and Soul Flame.
## Magi Knight

### Pattern
Starts with Power Shield, followed by Dampen.
Then repeatedly uses Ram, Prep, and Magic Bomb in that order.

## Strategy Notes

<!-- Reserved for the meta-agent. Append lessons, combos, and heuristics here.
     Wiki facts live above the divider. -->
- Magi's Dampen downgrades the whole deck while it lives; kill or nearly kill Magi before its Dampen turn when possible.
- Spectral's Hex makes all cards Ethereal while it lives; kill Spectral soon after Magi, especially in decks with key powers.
- Flail can usually be left for last, but Strength buffs make delayed cleanup dangerous without block scaling.
