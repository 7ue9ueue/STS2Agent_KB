# Doormaker

## Overview

The **Doormaker** is a Boss found in Glory. The fight starts with the **Doormaker** appearing as the Door with infinite health. It then transforms into the **Doormaker** on its first turn.

## Stats

### Doormaker
- **HP**: 489 (Asc8+: 512)
- **Appears in**: Debut Glory

### Attacks

| Name | Intent | Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Dramatic Open |  | Transforms into the Doormaker. Sets current buff to Hunger. |
| Hunger |  | Deals 30 damage. Swaps current buff to Scrutiny. |
| _(Asc variant)_ | | Deals 35 (Asc9+) damage. Swaps current buff to Scrutiny. |
| Scrutiny |  | Deals 24 damage. Swaps current buff to Grasp. |
| _(Asc variant)_ | | Deals 26 (Asc9+) damage. Swaps current buff to Grasp. |
| Grasp |  | Deals 10x2 damage. Gains 3 Strength. Swaps current buff to Hunger. |
| _(Asc variant)_ | | Deals 11x2 (Asc9+) damage. Gains 3 Strength. Swaps current buff to Hunger. |

## Pattern

The Doormaker uses Dramatic Open on its first turn, then follows a repeating cycle:
1. Hunger
1. Scrutiny
1. Grasp
## Notes

*Even though the HP bar in the first phase shows as "infinity", it is actually set to the finite number of 999,999,999 HP. As such, it is theoretically possible to kill the Doormaker in its first phase.

## Strategy Notes

<!-- Reserved for the meta-agent. Append lessons, combos, and heuristics here.
     Wiki facts live above the divider. -->
- 161-win corpus: common Act 3 boss (100 fights), averaging 31.3 damage and 7.3 turns.
- Adequate block plus the common draw/energy shell is usually enough; avoid entering depleted after the first Act 3 boss.
- Do not spend first-debuff relics on the infinite-HP Door; save doubled Vulnerable/Weak for Doormaker after Dramatic Open.
- Barricade plus Unmovable can outscale the damage cycle; bank block before Grasp, then convert it with Body Slam.
- Scrutiny blocks extra draw, so play draw cards before Scrutiny turns or treat them as block-only cards.
- At low HP, avoid installing Inferno unless its damage materially shortens the fight; the start-turn HP loss can turn otherwise survivable Grasp/Hunger lines into forced death.
- Doormaker still demands a damage clock; skipping Inferno/scaling entirely can let Strength ramps reach lethal Grasp turns even if early HP is preserved.
- Before ending Scrutiny at low HP, plan the next Grasp turn; its energy-loss tax can prevent combining small block with Weak, so prefer a single large block card, banked block, or pre-applied Weak.
- A purely defensive low-damage line can still lose by the second Grasp; pair deterministic block with early Vulnerable/burst or controlled scaling so the fight does not reach high-Strength cycles.
- When using Inferno and Blood Wall together, preserve enough HP for the post-Hunger Scrutiny turn; surviving Hunger at 1-3 HP can still be losing before the next action.
- Pre-applying Weak on a Scrutiny turn can make the following Grasp much easier to block because Grasp taxes each card played with energy loss.
- Liquid Memories on Flame Barrier can be correct on the first Scrutiny if it prevents a large HP leak while still allowing Vulnerable/scaling setup, but spending it early means the later Hunger/Scrutiny cycle needs a real HP cushion.
- If the fight reaches the second Hunger/Scrutiny cycle at 1-4 HP with no potion and no imminent lethal, treat the line as strategically losing even when the current turn can be blocked.
- Dark Embrace plus Anger can loop on Hunger turns when the live deck is thin: Anger is 0-cost, creates a copy, then Hunger exhausts the played copy and Dark Embrace draws. Scrutiny blocks this draw, and Grasp's energy tax can break card-spam lines.
- Dark Embrace plus Feel No Pain is a premier counterpackage because Hunger makes played Attacks/Skills exhaust, converting the boss tax into draw and block.
- Enter with a real damage-per-round engine, not only defensive cards. Strong plans include Vicious/Vulnerable burst, Barricade/Unmovable/Body Slam, Dark Embrace exhaust, or HP-safe Rupture/self-damage scaling.
