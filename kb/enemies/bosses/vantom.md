# Vantom

## Overview

The **Vantom** is a Boss found in the Overgrowth.

## Stats

### Vantom
- **HP**: 173 (Asc8+: 183)
- **Appears in**: Debut Overgrowth

### Attacks

| Name | Intent | Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Ink Blot |  | Deals 7 damage. |
| _(Asc variant)_ | | Deals 8 (Asc9+) damage. |
| Inky Lance |  | Deals 6 damage ×2. |
| _(Asc variant)_ | | Deals 7 (Asc9+) damage ×2. |
| Dismember |  | Deals 27 damage. Shuffles 3 Wound into your discard pile. |
| _(Asc variant)_ | | Deals 30 (Asc9+) damage. Shuffles 3 Wound into your discard pile. |
| Prepare |  | Gains 2 Strength. |

## Pattern

The Vantom cycles through its four moves in a fixed order, repeating forever:
1. Ink Blot
1. Inky Lance
1. Dismember
1. Prepare
## Notes

- The Vantom begins combat with 9 stacks of Slippery, which causes it to take only 1 HP of damage per hit for the first 9 times it loses HP. Multi-hit attacks are effective at removing Slippery stacks quickly.
- Prepare grants 2 Strength each cycle, so the Vantom's damage increases steadily over the fight.
- Dismember shuffles 3 Wound cards into your discard pile. Wound cards are Unplayable and clog your hand.

- 161-win corpus: high-damage boss in wins (36.1 avg, 39 median over 22 fights).

- Slippery (9): The Nth (last) hit still deals only 1 HP — you need N+1 hits total before real damage begins. Plan multi-hit attacks (Dismantle+, Thunderclap, Inky Lance Thorns) across T1–T2 to clear all 9 stacks.
