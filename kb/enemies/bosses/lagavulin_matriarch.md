# Lagavulin Matriarch

## Overview

**Lagavulin Matriarch** is a Boss found in the Underdocks.

## Stats

### Lagavulin Matriarch
- **HP**: 222 (Asc8+: 233)
- **Appears in**: Debut Underdocks

### Attacks

| Name | Intent | Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Sleep |  | Asleep. Does nothing. |
| Slash |  | Deals 19 damage. |
| _(Asc variant)_ | | Deals 21 (Asc9+) damage. |
| Disembowel |  | Deals 9 damage x2. |
| _(Asc variant)_ | | Deals 10 (Asc9+) damage x2. |
| Slash2 |  | Deals 12 damage. Gains 12 Block. |
| _(Asc variant)_ | | Deals 14 (Asc9+) damage. Gains 14 (Asc8+) Block. |
| Soul Siphon |  | Removes 2 Strength and 2 Dexterity from the player. Gains 2 Strength. |

## Pattern

The Lagavulin Matriarch starts Asleep with 12 Plating. It sleeps for 3 turns or until it takes unblocked damage, whichever comes first. When it wakes up, it loses its Plating.

After waking, it repeats this four-move cycle:
1. Slash
1. Disembowel
1. Slash2
1. Soul Siphon

- Waking the Lagavulin Matriarch early (by dealing unblocked damage) removes its Plating immediately, but also ends the free setup turns.
- Soul Siphon permanently reduces the player's Strength and Dexterity by 2 each cycle, making long fights increasingly punishing.
## Trivia

Lagavulin Matriarch's appearance, abilities, and name are a reference to the Lagavulin elite from Slay the Spire I.

## Strategy Notes

<!-- Reserved for the meta-agent. Append lessons, combos, and heuristics here.
     Wiki facts live above the divider. -->
- Use the three sleep turns for non-damaging setup such as Demon Form and hand upgrades; wake with burst on the final sleep turn to avoid early Plating turns.
- Non-damaging debuffs/setup such as Dominate do not wake it; Pyre+ is a strong final-campfire plan when the deck can draw it during sleep.
- After Soul Siphon, Defend-heavy hands can fail to close despite low boss HP; preserve potion or draw fixing to avoid another attack cycle.
- Do not let Sword Boomerang/other low-base multi-hit plans fight through repeated Soul Siphons; negative Strength can reduce hits to 0, and The Boot did not rescue 0-damage hits in live A10.
- If Vambrace is available, avoid spending it on sleep-turn overblock; saving it for the first real attack can turn Flame Barrier into 24 Block and preserve the HP needed to finish.
