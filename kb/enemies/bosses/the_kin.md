# The Kin

## Overview

**The Kin** is a Boss encounter found in the Overgrowth. The fight consists of two Kin Followers and one Kin Priest.

## Stats

### Kin Priest
- **HP**: 190 (Asc8+: 199)
- **Appears in**: Debut Overgrowth

### Kin Follower
- **HP**: 58-59 (Asc8+: 62-63)
- **Appears in**: Debut Overgrowth

## Kin Priest

### Pattern
The Kin Priest cycles through four moves in a fixed order:
1. Orb of Frailty
1. Orb of Weakness
1. Soul Beam
1. Dark Ritual
## Kin Follower

### Pattern
Each Kin Follower cycles through three moves in a fixed order:
1. Quick Slash
1. Boomerang
1. Power Dance

The two Kin Followers start offset from each other: one begins with Quick Slash and the other begins with Power Dance.
## Notes

- When both Kin Followers are killed, the Kin Priest has a dialogue reaction, but the fight continues normally.
- Both Kin Followers and the Kin Priest gain Strength through their buff moves (Power Dance and Dark Ritual), so the encounter's damage output scales over time.
- Orb of Frailty applies Frail, reducing your block gained by 25%. Orb of Weakness applies Weak, reducing your attack damage by 25%.
## Trivia

- A Kin Follower appears in Suppress's card art, being held by the Silent.
