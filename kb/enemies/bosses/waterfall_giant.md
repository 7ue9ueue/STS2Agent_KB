# Waterfall Giant

## Overview

**Waterfall Giant** is a Boss found in the Underdocks.

## Stats

### Waterfall Giant
- **HP**: 240 (Asc8+: 250)
- **Appears in**: Debut Underdocks

## Pattern

The Waterfall Giant uses Pressurize on its first turn, then follows a repeating cycle:
1. Stomp
1. Ram
1. Siphon
1. Pressure Gun
1. Pressure Up
1. Back to Stomp

Almost every move also adds 3 Steam Eruption. When the Waterfall Giant is killed while it has Steam Eruption, it becomes invulnerable and uses About To Blow, followed by Explode on the next turn, dealing damage equal to its accumulated Steam Eruption before dying.

- If the Waterfall Giant is killed when it has no Steam Eruption (e.g. via Doom), it dies normally without triggering the explosion sequence.
## Notes

*Even though the HP bar in the last phase shows as "infinity", it is actually set to the finite number of 999,999,999 HP. As such, it is theoretically possible to kill the Waterfall Giant in its last phase.
