# Kaiser Crab

## Overview

The **Kaiser Crab** is a Boss encounter found in Hive. It consists of two arms that fight simultaneously: the Crusher (left claw) and the Rocket (right claw). Players are Surrounded — attacks from behind deal 50% more damage depending on which arm the player is facing away from.

## Strategy Notes

<!-- Reserved for the meta-agent. Append lessons, combos, and heuristics here.
     Wiki facts live above the divider. -->
- 161-win corpus: among the highest-damage bosses even in wins (38.9 avg, 36.5 median over 44 fights).
- Enter with real block plus scaling; draw/energy alone is not enough if the fight drags.
- Prioritize the Rocket/Laser plan: Rocket's T4 Laser is the main danger turn, and Crusher's T3 Bug Sting can apply Frail/Weak immediately before it, making the Laser block check much harder.
- Enter with a specific Laser answer: Weak potion/effect on Rocket, heavy single-turn block, retained block, Intangible/Buffer-style prevention, or enough burst to kill/disable Rocket before T4.
- Kill Rocket first by default; it scales into the most dangerous live attacks, and leaving it alive after Crusher dies can make the Rage turn lethal.
- Exhaust-focused Ironclad decks should prioritize thinning quickly in this fight; a smaller deck cycles Bloodletting, Second Wind, Dark Embrace, and key attacks fast enough to reach repeatable burst or infinites.
- Use targeted cards to face the active attacker; a free generated attack can turn Rocket's Precision Beam from a back-attack hit into a survivable hit.
- Do not kill one claw before the other claw's live attack unless ready for the +6 Strength and 99 Block Rage swing; leaving a 1-2 HP claw alive during Recharge/Sleep can be correct.
