# Test Subject

## Overview

**Test Subject** is a Boss found in Glory. It has three phases, reviving twice with increasing HP and new powers.

## Stats

### Test Subject
- **HP**: 100 (Asc8+: 111)
- **Appears in**: Debut Glory

### Test Subject (Phase 3)

## Phase 1

### Pattern
Starts with Adaptable and Enrage 2 (). Alternates between Bite and Skull Bash.

When reduced to 0 HP, Adaptable prevents death and it revives into Phase 2.
## Phase 2

### Pattern
Revives with 200 () HP and gains Painful Stabs. Repeats Multi-Claw every turn. Multi-Claw starts at 3 hits and gains +1 hit each use.

When reduced to 0 HP again, it revives into Phase 3.
## Phase 3

### Pattern
Revives with 300 () HP, gains Nemesis, and loses Adaptable and Painful Stabs. Cycles through:
1. Lacerate
1. Big Pounce
1. Burning Growl
1. Back to Lacerate

This is the final phase — the Test Subject can now be killed permanently.
## Notes

- Each of Test Subject's phases will lose all status effects when transitioning to the next phase, including the Strength gained from Enrage in the first phase.
- Cards with Fatal triggers (such as Feed, The Hunt, & Hand of Greed.) will trigger successfully during _any phase_ of the Test Subject's.
    - Because the Test Subject is a Boss in Act 3, after which there are no opportunities to spend gold or earn card rewards, Feed is currently the only card with a Fatal trigger that matters.
- After Test Subject loses all its HP in a phase, it cannot be targeted by any cards until it revives into the next phase.
## Trivia

- Each phase of the Test Subject's boss battle is based on an Elite enemy from the original Slay the Spire:
    - Phase 1's Enrage buff is shared with the Act 1 elite Gremlin Nob. The Test Subject's claws and the spikes on its back also resemble the Gremlin Nob's horns.
    - Phase 2's Painful Stabs buff is shared with the Act 2 elite Book of Stabbing.
    - Phase 3's Nemesis buff is identical to the ability of the Act 3 elite Nemesis, and its icon depicts the mask worn by Nemesis.

- 161-win corpus: most common Act 3 boss in wins (115 fights), averaging 34.9 damage and 8.9 turns.
- Multi-phase fight rewards draw, repeated block, and reliable damage more than one-time burst.
- Phase 2 currently repeats Multi-Claw every turn; prioritize block or a burst phase-skip before hit count climbs.
- Gigantification Potion and other triple-damage effects are excellent on a large single Attack during Phase 2/3, especially after Vulnerable.
- Use Phase 3 Intangible turns for setup powers/block; save burst for non-Intangible turns.
- Treat Phase 2 as the primary scaling deadline; if the deck cannot repeatedly block Multi-Claw, save Vulnerable/Strength/Gigantification/Fiend Fire/Whirlwind/Body Slam burst to skip it.
- Dark Embrace plus Feel No Pain and controlled exhaust stays strong across all three phases because Burns/statuses become draw and block instead of deck pollution.
- Demon Form, Rupture, Vicious, Barricade, and Unmovable are higher value here than in hallway fights because the multi-phase structure gives scaling time to pay off.
