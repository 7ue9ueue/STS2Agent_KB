# Bosses

**[STUB]** Wiki page is marked incomplete — verify numbers at source before relying on them.

## Overview

quiet

Bosses are extremely difficult monsters that are fought at the end of each act. Compared to normal enemies and elites, bosses have significantly more health and a more powerful move set that usually involves special gimmicks that cripple the player or heavily buffs itself. Every boss has a unique portrait shown at the top of the Spire map.

The map location prior to a boss fight will always be a Rest Site, allowing the player to heal before the fight. The exception is the double Boss fight at the end of Act 3 when playing on  where the second boss is fought directly after the first one.



## Ascension Modifiers

- : All Enemies have more HP.
- : All Enemies deal more damage.
- : Two bosses are fought in a row at the end of Act 3.
## Rewards

Upon defeating the Act 1 and Act 2 bosses, the player is rewarded with 100 () Gold, a choice between three Rare Cards, and sometimes a random Potion. Afterwards, the player is sent to the start of the next Act, where they encounter an Ancient who heals the player for 100% () of their missing health. The Ancient then gives the player a choice between three of their unique Ancient Relics.
## Interactions

### Relics
- Pantograph: Heals for 25 HP at the start of a Boss combat.
- Stone Cracker: At the start of Boss combats, Upgrade 3 random cards in your Draw Pile for the rest of combat.
- Lava Rock: The Act 1 Boss drops 2 Relics.
