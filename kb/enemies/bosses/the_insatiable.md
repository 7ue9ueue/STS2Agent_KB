# The Insatiable

## Overview

**The Insatiable** is a Boss found in Hive.

## Stats

### The Insatiable
- **HP**: 321 (Asc8+: 341)
- **Appears in**: Debut Hive

### Attacks

| Name | Intent | Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Liquify Ground |  | Applies 4 Sandpit to each player. Shuffles 6 Frantic Escape into each player's deck (3 into draw pile, 3 into discard pile). |
| Thrash |  | Deals 8 damage ×2. |
| _(Asc variant)_ | | Deals 9 (Asc9+) damage ×2. |
| Lunging Bite |  | Deals 28 damage. |
| _(Asc variant)_ | | Deals 31 (Asc9+) damage. |
| Salivate |  | Gains 2 Strength. |
| _(Asc variant)_ | | Gains 3 (Asc9+) Strength. |

## Pattern

The Insatiable always opens with Liquify Ground, then repeats the following cycle:
1. Thrash
1. Lunging Bite
1. Salivate
1. Thrash
1. Back to Lunging Bite

## Strategy Notes

<!-- Reserved for the meta-agent. Append lessons, combos, and heuristics here.
     Wiki facts live above the divider. -->
- 161-win corpus: averages 28.9 damage and 6.7 turns over 55 fights.
- Sandpit is the primary threat; the attacks are usually manageable if you keep distance high. Play 1-cost Frantic Escapes early instead of letting them become expensive.
- Sandpit decrements each enemy phase. With 6 FEs (starting cost 1), plan to play roughly 1 FE/turn. Missing 1 turn is safe if Sandpit≥3; missing 2 consecutive turns risks death once Sandpit≤2.
- Salivate (wiki) = "Empower" (Buff intent in-game) — fully free offense turn, skip all defense.
- Weak expires at START of the enemy's own attack phase, not end of turn — Weak=1 gives NO benefit on the enemy's attack that turn.
- Howl from Beyond+ does NOT exhaust when played from hand; it goes to discard and must enter exhaust via another method to trigger auto-play.
- T2 (Candelabra 8e): Use for Impervious (30 Block vs Lunging Bite 28) + damage setup. Lunging Bite is T3 in the cycle.
- Fights scale hard after Salivate: each Salivate gives +2 Str permanently. Kill by T7-8 before Strength stacks make attacks unsurvivable.
- Avoid skipping Frantic Escape at Sandpit 2 unless lethal is guaranteed; next-turn Escape may cost 2-3 and become unaffordable after blocking.
- Barricade/Unmovable plus large block cards can convert late survival into lethal Body Slam damage, useful when Sandpit pressure prevents slow chip lines.
- Late-fight Frantic Escape costs can reach 3+; at Sandpit 2, prioritize playing an affordable Escape before spending energy on block unless lethal is guaranteed.
