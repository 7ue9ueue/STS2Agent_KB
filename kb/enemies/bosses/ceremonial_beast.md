## Pattern

The Ceremonial Beast has two phases.

**Phase 1:** Uses Stamp on the first turn, gaining Plow. Then uses Plow every turn. When its HP drops to the Plow threshold (150, or ), it becomes Stunned and loses all Strength.

**Phase 2:** After being stunned, repeats this three-move cycle:
1. Beast Cry
1. Stomp
1. Crush
## Notes

- Plow acts as a HP threshold: when the Ceremonial Beast's HP drops to the Plow amount or below, it is stunned and loses all accumulated Strength. This effectively gives the player a free turn.
- Ringing from Beast Cry limits the player to playing only 1 card on that turn.
- During Phase 1, each Plow grants 2 Strength, but all of it is lost when the stun triggers. In Phase 2, Crush grants 3 () Strength that is retained permanently.
