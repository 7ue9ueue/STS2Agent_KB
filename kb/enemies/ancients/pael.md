## Overview

quietPael
Pael is one of the Ancients in Slay the Spire 2. He is one of the possible Ancients for the start of Act 2.

## Effective Probability

- All pool 1 Relics always have a 33.3% chance of being offered.
If you have at least 3 Enchantable Defends and at least 5 removable cards:
- Pael's Growth has a 14.3% chance of being offered, and all other pool 2 Relics have a 28.6% chance of being offered.
If you have less than 3 Enchantable Defends and at least 5 removable cards:
- Pael's Claw will not be offered.
- Pael's Growth has a 20% chance of being offered, and Pael's Wing and Pael's Tooth each have a 40% chance.
If you have at least 3 Enchantable Defends and less than 5 removable cards:
- Pael's Tooth will not be offered.
- Pael's Growth has a 20% chance of being offered, and Pael's Wing and Pael's Claw each have a 40% chance.
If you have less than 3 Enchantable Defends and less than 5 removable cards:
- Pael's Tooth and Pael's Claw will not be offered.
- Pael's Growth has a 33.3% chance of being offered, and Pael's Wing has a 66.6% chance.
If you do not have access to a "pet" (don't have Byrdpip or Byrdonis Egg) yet:
- All pool 3 Relics have a 33.3% chance of being offered.
If you have Byrdpip or Byrdonis Egg:
- Pael's Legion will not be offered.
- Pael's Blood and Pael's Eye each have a 50% chance of being offered.
