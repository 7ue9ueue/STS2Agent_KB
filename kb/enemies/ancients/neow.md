# Neow

## Overview

quietNeow
Neow is one of the Ancients in Slay the Spire 2. She is unlocked after playing the first run of a save file and will subsequently always appear at the start of a new run, allowing you to choose one of 3 boons.



## Choices

Her Relic choices are divided into 2 pools: a Positive pool without downsides, and a Curse pool with downsides for all the Relics. One Relic out of the Curse pool is chosen first, then the Positive pool is adjusted. After that, the previously chosen Curse Relic and two Relics out of the Positive pool are offered to the player.

## Effective Probability

If not playing Multiplayer:
- All Curse pool Relics have a 12.5% chance of being offered.
- Massive Scroll will not be offered.
- Lava Rock and Small Capsule have a 7% chance of being offered.
- Nutritious Oyster, Stone Humidifier, Neow's Talisman, and Pomander have an 8% chance of being offered.
- Arcane Scroll, Golden Pearl, New Leaf, and Precise Scissors have a 14% chance of being offered.
- All other Positive pool Relics have a 15.9% chance of being offered.

If playing Multiplayer:
- Silver Crucible will not be offered.
- All other Curse pool Relics have a 14.3% chance of being offered.
- Lava Rock and Small Capsule have a 6.4% chance of being offered.
- Nutritious Oyster, Stone Humidifier, Neow's Talisman, and Pomander have a 7.5% chance of being offered.
- Arcane Scroll, Golden Pearl, New Leaf, and Precise Scissors have a 12.9% chance of being offered.
- All other Positive pool Relics have a 15.1% chance of being offered.
