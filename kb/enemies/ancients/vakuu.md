# Vakuu

**[STUB]** Wiki page is marked incomplete — verify numbers at source before relying on them.

## Overview

quietVakuu
Vakuu is one of the Ancients in Slay the Spire 2. He is one of the possible Ancients for the start of Act 3.



## Choices

His Relic choices are divided into 3 pools, where 1 Relic out of each pool is offered to the player.

### Pool 1
{| class="wikitable"
! Relic
! Description
|-
| Blood-Soaked Rose|| Upon pickup, add 1 Enthralled to your Deck. Gain  at the start of each turn.
|-
| Fiddle|| At the start of each turn, draw 2 additional cards. You may not draw cards during your turn.
|-
| Whispering Earring|| Gain  at the start of each turn. Vakuu plays your first turn for you.
|}
### Pool 2
{| class="wikitable"
! Relic
! Description
|-
| Distinguished Cape|| Upon pickup, lose 9 Max HP. Add 3 Apparitions to your Deck.
|-
| Preserved Fog|| Upon pickup, remove 3 cards from your Deck. Add Folly to your Deck.
|-
| Sere Talon|| Upon pickup, add 2 random Curses (from a certain pool) and 3 Wishes to your Deck.
|}

### Pool 3
{| class="wikitable"
! Relic
! Description
|-
| Choices Paradox|| At the start of each combat, add 1 of 5 random cards into your Hand. Add Retain to the chosen card.
|-
| Jeweled Mask|| At the start of combat put a random Power from your Draw Pile into your Hand, it's free to play.
|-
| Lord's Parasol|| When you encounter the Merchant, immediately obtain EVERYTHING he sells.
|-
| Music Box|| Create an Ethereal copy of the first Attack you play each turn.
|}
## Effective Probability

- All pool 1 and pool 2 Relics have a 33.3% chance of being offered.
- All pool 3 Relics have a 25% chance of being offered.
