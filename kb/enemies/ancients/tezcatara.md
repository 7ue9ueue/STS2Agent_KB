# Tezcatara

**[STUB]** Wiki page is marked incomplete — verify numbers at source before relying on them.

## Overview

quietTezcatara
Tezcatara is one of the Ancients in Slay the Spire 2. It is one of the possible Ancients for the start of Act 2.



## Choices

Its relic choices are divided into 3 pools, where 1 relic out of each pool is offered to the player.

## Strategy Notes
