# The Architect

**[STUB]** Wiki page is marked incomplete — verify numbers at source before relying on them.

## Overview

Spoiler warning
The Architect

The Architect is one of the Ancients in Slay the Spire 2, he is the final Ancient encountered at the end of Act 3, and reaching him signals the end of the the run. The player characters are tasked with killing the Architect by Neow, but this is not currently possible within the game. Instead, players deal damage to the Architect based on the run's score, and then they are killed, being sent back to Neow to try again.



## Damage Formula

The damage dealt to the Architect is equal to the player's score. More information can be found at Score.
