# Darv

## Overview

quietDarv
Darv is one of the Ancients in Slay the Spire 2. He is the only Ancient who can appear in Act 2 or Act 3.



## Choices

His Relic choices are divided into 2 pools. There is a 50% chance you are offered 3 Relics out of pool 1, and a 50% chance for only 2 pool 1 Relics to be offered, the last Relic being the only option out of pool 2: Dusty Tome.

## Effective Probability

- Dusty Tome always has a 50% chance of being offered.
In normal runs, if you are in Act 2:
- Philosopher's Stone and Velvet Choker will not be offered.
- All other pool 1 Relics have a 27.8% chance of being offered.
In normal runs, if you are in Act 3:
- Ectoplasm and Sozu will not be offered.
- All other pool 1 Relics have a 27.8% chance of being offered.
In Custom Mode runs with the Draft, Sealed Deck, or Insanity modifier(s), if you are in Act 2:
- Pandora's Box, Philosopher's Stone, and Velvet Choker will not be offered.
- All other pool 1 Relics have a 31.3% chance of being offered.
In Custom Mode runs with the Draft, Sealed Deck, or Insanity modifier(s), if you are in Act 3:
- Pandora's Box, Ectoplasm and Sozu will not be offered.
- All other pool 1 Relics have a 31.3% chance of being offered.
