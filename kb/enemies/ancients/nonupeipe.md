## Overview

quietNonupeipe
Nonupeipe is one of the Ancients in Slay the Spire 2. They are one of the possible Ancients for the start of Act 3.

## Effective Probability

If you have at least 4 cards that can be Enchanted with Swift:
- All Relics have a 30% chance of being offered.

If you do not have at least 4 cards that can be Enchanted with Swift:
- Beautiful Bracelet will not be offered.
- All other Relics have a 33.3% chance of being offered.
