## Overview

quietOrobas
Orobas is one of the Ancients in Slay the Spire 2. Orobas is one of the possible Ancients for the start of Act 2.

## Effective Probability

- Prismatic Gem has an 8.3% chance of being offered, and Sea Glass has a 16.7% chance. All other pool 1 Relics have a 25% chance of being offered.
- All pool 2 Relics have a 33.3% chance of being offered.
- Archaic Tooth and Touch of Orobas each have a 50% chance of being offered.
