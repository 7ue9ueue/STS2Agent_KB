## Strategy Notes

<!-- Reserved for the meta-agent. Append lessons, combos, and heuristics here.
     Wiki facts live above the divider. -->
- Pattern (Asc 0): Vomit Ichor (10 Slimed, T1) → Furious Pummeling (4×4=16) → Leeching Hug (3 Weak + 3 Str, NO damage) → Smother (30 base, 33 at Str 3) → repeat. T3 Leeching Hug is a completely free offense turn.
- Smother is the critical danger hit (33 dmg at Str 3). Need 30+ Block or Colossus+ (50% less from Vul enemy = ~17 actual). Plan Block access before T4.
- Weak (3 stacks from Leeching Hug) reduces Smother to ~25 and Furious Pummeling next cycle — track expiry (fires at start of enemy turn).
- Prep Time+ (Vigor) on free turns (Vomit Ichor or Leeching Hug) sets up burst kills on subsequent offensive turns.
- Juggernaut + CM + Tuning Fork passive chip can deal 14+ damage at turn start by mid-fight, enabling kill setups below 20 HP.
- The 10 Slimed cards can flood the first reshuffle; prioritize clean burst before the first shuffle or have exhaust/draw ready.
