# Scroll of Biting

## Overview

**Scroll of Biting** is a normal Enemy found in Glory. It appears in groups of 3 (weak) or 4 (normal), each starting on a different move.

## Stats

### Scroll of Biting
- **HP**: 31-38 (Asc8+: 32-39)
- **Appears in**: Debut Glory

### Attacks

| Name | Intent | Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Chomp |  | Deals 14 damage. |
| _(Asc variant)_ | | Deals 16 (Asc9+) damage. |
| Chew |  | Deals 5 damage 2 times. |
| _(Asc variant)_ | | Deals 6 (Asc9+) damage 2 times. |
| More Teeth |  | Gains 2 Strength. |

## Pattern

Each Scroll of Biting starts on a different move in the cycle. The pattern is: Chomp, then More Teeth, then Chew.

After Chew, it randomly picks either Chomp (cannot repeat) or Chew (weight 2), then continues the cycle from that point.
## In Party With

### Glory
- 3 Scrolls of Biting (Weak)
- 4 Scrolls of Biting

## Strategy Notes

<!-- Reserved for the meta-agent. Append lessons, combos, and heuristics here.
     Wiki facts live above the divider. -->
- Fully block or kill attacking Scrolls first; even 1 unblocked damage triggers Paper Cuts max HP loss.
- High-energy AoE can be better than blocking if it kills every Scroll before Paper Cuts can trigger.
- If perfect block is impossible, concentrate damage on the attacker that will leak after left-to-right block consumption, then repair the fight with Feed/max-HP value when available.
- Against 4 Scrolls, do not sacrifice Barricade/retained-block setup lightly; exhausting Howl from Beyond can be better because it auto-plays next turn and supplies repeated AoE.
