## Strategy Notes

<!-- Reserved for the meta-agent. Append lessons, combos, and heuristics here.
     Wiki facts live above the divider. -->
- T1 = Forbidden Incantation (no attack). Ritual does NOT fire on the turn it's applied — first Str gain is at end of T2.
- Attack escalation: T2=12, T3=21, T4=30, T5=39, T6=48. Must kill by T5 to avoid unsurvivable damage.
- Passive relic chip (Mr. Struggles + Mercury Hourglass) deals 8 total at T5 start — plan your kill turn using that pre-damage.
- Powers (Vicious, Stone Armor) count as Skills for Tuning Fork counter.
- Do not spend exhaust-only defensive bombs on turn 1 unless they meaningfully accelerate lethal; the real danger starts after Ritual has stacked Strength.
