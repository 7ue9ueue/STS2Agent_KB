# The Lost and Forgotten

## Overview

**The Lost and Forgotten** are a pair of normal Enemies found in Glory. They always appear together.

## Stats

### The Lost
- **HP**: 93 (Asc8+: 99)
- **Appears in**: Debut Glory

### The Forgotten
- **HP**: 106 (Asc8+: 111)
- **Appears in**: Debut Glory

### Attacks

| Name | Intent | Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Debilitating Smog |  | Removes 2 Strength from the player and gains 2 Strength. |
| Eye Lasers |  | Deals 4 damage x2. |
| _(Asc variant)_ | | Deals 5 (Asc9+) damage x2. |

### Attacks

| Name | Intent | Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Miasma |  | Removes 2 Dexterity from the player. Gains 8 Block and 2 Dexterity. |
| Dread |  | Deals 13 damage plus the amount of Dexterity the Forgotten has. |
| _(Asc variant)_ | | Deals 15 (Asc9+) damage plus the amount of Dexterity the Forgotten has.. |

## The Lost

### Pattern
Alternates between Debilitating Smog and Eye Lasers, starting with Debilitating Smog.
## The Forgotten

### Pattern
Alternates between Miasma and Dread, starting with Miasma.
