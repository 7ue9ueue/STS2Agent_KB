# Ruby Raiders

## Overview

The **Ruby Raiders** are a group of normal Enemies found in the Overgrowth. Each encounter features 3 raiders randomly chosen from a pool of 5 types.

## Stats

### Tracker Raider
- **HP**: 21-25 (Asc8+: 22-26)

## Axe Raider

### Pattern
Repeats a fixed cycle:
- Swing
- Swing
- Big Swing
## Assassin Raider

### Pattern
Uses Killshot every turn.
## Brute Raider

### Pattern
Alternates between Beat and Clap, starting with Beat.
## Crossbow Raider

### Pattern
Alternates between Reload and Fire!, starting with Reload.
## Tracker Raider

### Pattern
Opens with Track, then uses Unleash the Hounds every turn after.
## Notes

- The encounter always contains exactly 3 raiders chosen at random from the 5 types, with no duplicates.
- The Tracker Raider's Track applies Frail, which reduces Block gained by 25%. This makes Unleash the Hounds and other raiders' attacks harder to block.
- The Brute Raider gains 3 Strength every other turn from Clap, causing its Beat damage to scale over time.
- The Crossbow Raider only attacks every other turn, but Fire! deals 14 damage in a single hit. On Reload turns, it gains only 3 Block.
