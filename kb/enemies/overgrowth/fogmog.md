# Fogmog

## Overview

**Fogmog** is a normal Enemy found in the Overgrowth. It summons an Eye With Teeth at the start of the fight.

## Stats

### Fogmog
- **HP**: 74 (Asc8+: 78)
- **Appears in**: Debut Overgrowth

### Eye With Teeth
- **HP**: 6
- **Appears in**: Debut Overgrowth

## Fogmog

### Pattern
Fogmog always opens with Illusory Spores to summon an Eye With Teeth, then uses Thwack.

After that, it randomly picks Thwack (40%) or Headbutt (60%), and cannot pick the same one twice in a row. After a random Thwack, it always follows up with Headbutt. After Headbutt, it always follows up with Thwack, then returns to the random pick.
## Eye With Teeth

The Eye With Teeth is a minion summoned by Fogmog via Illusory Spores.

### Pattern
Uses Distract every turn, shuffling 3 Dazed into your discard pile.
## Notes

- The Eye With Teeth has only 6 HP but has Illusion, so it revives at full HP the turn after it is destroyed. This means it will keep coming back and adding Dazed cards to your deck unless you kill Fogmog first.
- Fogmog gains 1 Strength every time it uses Thwack, so its damage steadily increases over the fight.
- Prioritizing Fogmog over the Eye With Teeth is generally the best strategy, since the Eye With Teeth will revive anyway and Fogmog's Strength scaling makes the fight harder the longer it goes.
- Dazed cards from Distract are Unplayable and clog your hand, reducing the number of useful cards you draw.
