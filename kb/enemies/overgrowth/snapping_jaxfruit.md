# Snapping Jaxfruit

## Overview

**Snapping Jaxfruit** is a normal Enemy found in the Overgrowth.

## Stats

### Snapping Jaxfruit
- **HP**: 31-33 (Asc8+: 34-36)
- **Appears in**: Debut Overgrowth

### Attacks

| Name | Intent | Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Energy Orb |  | Deals 3 damage. Gains 2 Strength. |
| _(Asc variant)_ | | Deals 4 (Asc9+) damage. Gains 2 Strength. |

## Pattern

Uses Energy Orb every turn.
## Notes

- Although its starting damage is low, the Strength accumulation means it becomes increasingly threatening if left alive.
