# Slimes

## Overview

**Slimes** are normal Enemies found in the Overgrowth. They come in four varieties: two Leaf Slimes and two Twig Slimes, each in a small (S) and medium (M) size. They appear together in the Slimes encounter and as companions in other Overgrowth encounters.

## Stats

### Leaf Slime (S)
- **HP**: 11-15 (Asc8+: 12-16)
- **Appears in**: Debut Overgrowth

### Leaf Slime (M)
- **HP**: 32-35 (Asc8+: 33-36)
- **Appears in**: Debut Overgrowth

### Twig Slime (S)
- **HP**: 7-11 (Asc8+: 8-12)
- **Appears in**: Debut Overgrowth

### Twig Slime (M)
- **HP**: 26-28 (Asc8+: 27-29)
- **Appears in**: Debut Overgrowth

## Encounters

- Easy Slimes: 1 of each (S) Slime, and a Random (M) Slime.
- Hard Slimes: 1 of each Slime variety.
- Flyconid and a Random (M) Slime.
- Slithering Strangler and 1 of each (S) Slime.
- Slithering Strangler and a Random (M) Slime.
## Leaf Slime (S)

### Pattern
Randomly picks Tackle or Goop each turn. Cannot use the same move twice in a row.
## Leaf Slime (M)

### Pattern
Always starts with Sticky Shot, then alternates between Clump Shot and Sticky Shot.
## Twig Slime (S)

### Pattern
Uses Tackle every turn.
## Twig Slime (M)

### Pattern
Always starts with Sticky Shot. After that, each turn it randomly picks Chomp (67%) or Sticky Shot (33%). Cannot use Sticky Shot twice in a row.
