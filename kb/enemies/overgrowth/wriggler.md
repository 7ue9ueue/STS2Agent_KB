# Wriggler

## Overview

**Wriggler** is a normal Enemy found in the Overgrowth. It appears in the Dense Vegetation event and is also summoned by Phrog Parasite upon its death.

## Stats

### Wriggler
- **HP**: 17-21 (Asc8+: 18-22)
- **Appears in**: Debut Overgrowth

### Attacks

| Name | Intent | Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Nasty Bite |  | Deals 6 damage. |
| _(Asc variant)_ | | Deals 7 (Asc9+) damage. |
| Wriggle |  | Shuffles 1 Infection into your discard pile. Gains 2 Strength. |

## Pattern

Wrigglers alternate between Nasty Bite and Wriggle. When multiple Wrigglers are present, they start offset from each other: odd-numbered Wrigglers begin with Nasty Bite and even-numbered Wrigglers begin with Wriggle.

When summoned by Phrog Parasite (via Infested), each Wriggler spends its first turn stunned before beginning its pattern.
## Notes

- In the Dense Vegetation event, 4 Wrigglers appear at once, starting their patterns immediately (not stunned).
- When summoned by Phrog Parasite, all 4 Wrigglers are stunned for their first turn, giving the player one turn to prepare.
- Wriggle both adds an Infection status card to your deck and grants the Wriggler 2 Strength, making its Nasty Bite hit harder each cycle.
- With 4 Wrigglers alternating, you take multiple attacks and status cards per turn. Removing Wrigglers quickly reduces incoming damage and deck pollution.
