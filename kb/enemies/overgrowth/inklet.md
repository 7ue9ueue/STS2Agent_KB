# Inklet

## Overview

**Inklet** is a normal Enemy found in the Overgrowth. They always appear in groups of three and start with one stack of Slippery each.

## Stats

### Inklet
- **HP**: 11-17 (Asc8+: 12-18)
- **Appears in**: Debut Overgrowth

### Attacks

| Name | Intent | Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Jab |  | Deals 3 damage. |
| _(Asc variant)_ | | Deals 4 (Asc9+) damage. |
| Windup Punch |  | Deals 2 damage ×3. |
| _(Asc variant)_ | | Deals 3 (Asc9+) damage ×3. |
| Piercing Gaze |  | Deals 10 damage. |
| _(Asc variant)_ | | Deals 11 (Asc9+) damage. |

## Pattern

The two outer Inklets open with Jab (most likely) or Windup Punch. The middle Inklet always opens with Windup Punch.

Each Inklet will alternate between Jab and a random choice of either Piercing Gaze or Windup Punch. After either Windup Punch or Piercing Gaze, the Inklet always uses Jab. After Jab, it will randomly choose between Piercing Gaze and Windup Punch.
## Trivia

- One of Vantom's death messages implies that Inklets are humans transformed into monsters by Vantom.

## Strategy Notes

<!-- Reserved for the meta-agent. Append lessons, combos, and heuristics here.
     Wiki facts live above the divider. -->
- Strip Slippery with low-value hits before committing high-damage attacks; otherwise large attacks collapse to 1 HP loss.
- The middle Inklet's opening multi-hit is a good first kill target, but remove outer Slippery before their Piercing Gaze/Windup follow-up turn.
- At very low HP, prevent both outer Inklets from reaching Piercing Gaze together; stripping and killing one outer can be safer than tunneling the middle Inklet.
