## Pathing Rules

- Elites are worth relics when the deck has the correct preparation, not just high HP.
- Act 1 elites ask for solid frontloaded damage.
- Act 2 Hive elites need a higher bar because Decimillipede can punish weak AoE and weak block/draw.
- Act 3 elites should be judged against the final boss plan; skip optional elites that consume the potion or HP needed for the boss.

## High-Risk Checks

- [Decimillipede](../enemies/elites/decimillipede.md): avoid without AoE, multi-target damage, or a solid block/draw engine; staggered kills can revive segments.
- [Terror Eel](../enemies/elites/terror_eel.md): plan around the Screech/Vulnerable window and save burst or potions.
- [Soul Nexus](../enemies/elites/soul_nexus.md): enter with enough HP and block for Drain Life into a possible Vulnerable-boosted attack.
