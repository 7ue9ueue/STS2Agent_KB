## Card Text

**Base**: Deal 5 damage twice. Increase the damage of ALL Maul cards by 1 this combat.



## Notes

### Notes

- This card is functionally similar to Claw, where it will increase the damage of all other copies of itself, except **Maul** starts with higher base damage, and increases damage by 1 less than Claw.

### Obtaining Maul

Up to 6 of your cards can be Transformed into Maul upon taking Claws from Tanx's offering at the start of Act 3.
*Transforming upgraded cards will grant upgraded copies of Maul.
*Transforming Enchanted cards will also grant copies of Maul with the same Enchantment(s), if Maul is eligible for the Enchantment.
