## Card Text

**Base**: Gain an additional 5 Block from Defend cards.



## Notes

### Interactions

=== Synergies ===
====Cards====
*Fasten provides additional Block for Ultimate Defends as well.
====Enchantments====
*Defends with Adroit benefit from Fasten twofold: Their core Block _and_ additional Block from Adroit will both be buffed.
=== Neutral ===
Fasten's additional Block is not affected by Dexterity.
===Anti-Synergies===
- Obtaining Spiked Gauntlets from Tanx raises the cost of this card by 1.

### Obtaining Fasten

Fasten is a Colorless card, and therefore can be obtained in the following ways:
- Can be bought from the Merchant.
- Can be obtained from Monsters and Elites via Dingy Rug.
- Can appear in your starting Deck in the Daily Climb with the All Star modifier.
- Can be obtained from Lead Paperweight.
- Can be obtained from a card reward from the  option at Brain Leech.
- Can be obtained from the  option at Endless Conveyor if Fried Eel is offered.
