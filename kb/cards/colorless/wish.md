## Card Text

**Upgraded**: Retain . Put a card from your Draw Pile into your Hand. Exhaust .


## Notes

### Obtaining Wish

3 Wishes can be obtained by taking Sere Talon from Vakuu's offering at the start of Act 3, also obtaining 2 random Curses.
*The Curses added will never include Ascender's Bane, Bad Luck, Curse of the Bell, Enthralled, Folly, Greed, Poor Sleep, or Spore Mind.
