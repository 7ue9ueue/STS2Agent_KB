## Card Text

**Base**: Deal 4 damage. Exhaust .

## Notes

### Interactions

- Accuracy: Shivs deal 4 additional damage.
- Phantom Blades: Shivs gain Retain. The first Shiv you play each turn deals 9 additional damage.
- Knife Trap: Play every Shiv in your Exhaust Pile on the enemy.
- Fan of Knives: Shivs now hit ALL enemies.
- Helical Dart: Whenever you play a Shiv, gain 1 Dexterity this turn.

=== Synergies ===
- As Shivs are 0- cost Attack cards that are usually generated in multiples, they synergize with:
    - Finisher, which increases in power for the number of Attacks played.
    - Envenom, which applies Poison with each Shiv.
    - Buffs/Debuffs activated when a card is played, including Strangle, Afterimage, Serpent Form, and another player's Sneaky.
    - Relics that trigger when Attacks are played, including Shuriken, Kunai, Ornamental Fan, Kusarigama, and Nunchaku.
- Anything that benefits from Exhaust will be triggered by playing Shivs.

=== Neutral ===
- If a Shiv generated by this card exceeds your hand size limit, it gets added to the discard pile.
- Because of their low base damage, any Strength gain will significantly increase their damage output.
    - Any negative Strength and Weak will also greatly mitigate damage from Shivs.

=== Anti-Synergy ===
- As multiple Shivs are often played in a single turn, they have an anti-synergy with:
    - Effects that limit card plays, including Velvet Choker and Ringing.
    - Effects that produce negative effects for played cards, including Thorns and Tender.
    - Tangled, which increases -cost of Shivs to 1.
