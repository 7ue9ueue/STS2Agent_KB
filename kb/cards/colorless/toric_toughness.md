## Card Text

**Base**: Gain 5 Block . Gain 5 Block at the start of the next 2 turns.

**Upgraded**: Gain 7 Block . Gain 7 Block at the start of the next 2 turns.


## Notes

### Interactions

=== Neutral ===
- Block gained from the Toric Toughness buff is affected by modifiers like Dexterity, Frail, Shadowmeld, and Vambrace at the moment of playing Toric Toughness, not at the moment of the Block taking effect. This means that the amount of Block gained at the start of the next two turns will always match the amount of Block gained immediately when playing Toric Toughness, even if that's 0 due to the No Block debuff.

### Obtaining Toric Toughness

- Toric Toughness is an Event Card, and the only way to obtain it is through the event Wood Carvings.
    - The  option allows you to choose a starter card to Transform into Toric Toughness.
