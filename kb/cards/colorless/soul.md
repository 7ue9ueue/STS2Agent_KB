## Card Text

**Base**: Draw 2 cards. Exhaust .



## Notes

### Interactions

- Haunt: Whenever you play a Soul, a random enemy loses 6(8) HP.
- Devour Life: Whenever you play a Soul, Summon 1(2).
- Soul Storm: Deals 2(3) additional damage for each Soul in your Exhaust Pile.

=== Synergies ===
- As Souls are 0- cost Skills cards that draw cards (possibly including other Souls), they synergize with:
    - Death March, which increases in power for each card drawn.
    - Buffs/Debuffs activated when a card is played, including Oblivion and another player's Sneaky.
    - Relics that trigger when Skills are played, including Letter Opener and Tuning Fork.
- Anything that benefits from Exhaust will be triggered by playing Souls.

=== Neutral ===

=== Anti-Synergy ===
- As Souls are often played frequently, they have an anti-synergy with:
    - Effects that limit card plays, including Velvet Choker and Ringing.
    - Effects that produce negative effects for played cards, such as Tender.
