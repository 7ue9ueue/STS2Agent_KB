## Card Text

**Base**: Deal 33 damage. Stun the enemy. Exhaust .



## Notes

### Notes

*The turn after being Stunned, the enemy will generally repeat the Intent that was interrupted.
    - If an enemy has already Stunned itself that turn, or if Whistle's damage triggers the enemy's Stun mechanic, Whistle's Stun will not affect the enemy's intents.
*Stuns the enemy even if it has Artifact, as Stun is not treated as a debuff.

### Obtaining Whistle

Can be obtained by taking Tanx's Whistle from Tanx's offering at the start of Act 3.
