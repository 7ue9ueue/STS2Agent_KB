## Card Text

**Base**: Deal 10 damage. Put 2 random cards from your Discard Pile into your Hand. Exhaust .

**Upgraded**: Deal 14 damage. Put 3 random cards from your Discard Pile into your Hand. Exhaust .


## Notes

### Obtaining Neow's Fury

Added to your deck after picking up Neow's Torment if offered by Neow at the start of a run.
