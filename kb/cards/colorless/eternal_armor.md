## Notes

### Interactions

===Synergies===
- Playing Eternal Armor with Mummified Hand in play will reduce a random card's cost to 0.
- Playing Eternal Armor with Game Piece in play will draw a card.
- Playing Eternal Armor with Lost Wisp deals 8 damage to ALL enemies.
*Intimidating Helmet grants 4 Block whenever Eternal Armor is played.
=== Anti-Synergies ===
- Obtaining Spiked Gauntlets from Tanx raises the cost of this card by 1.

### Obtaining Eternal Armor

Eternal Armor is a Colorless card, and therefore can be obtained in the following ways:
- Can be bought from the Merchant.
- Can be obtained from Monsters, Elites and Bosses with Dingy Rug.
    - Can also appear in your starting Deck in the Daily Climb with the All Star modifier.
- Can be obtained from Lead Paperweight.
- Can be obtained from a card reward from the  option at Brain Leech.
- Can be obtained from the  option at Endless Conveyor if Fried Eel is offered.
