## Card Text

**Base**: Deal 9 damage. Choose 1 of 3 cards in your Draw Pile to add into your Hand.

**Upgraded**: Deal 12 damage. Choose 1 of 3 cards in your Draw Pile to add into your Hand.


## Notes

### Interactions

=== Synergies ===
- Seeker Strike gets a damage increase of 3 from Strike Dummy, and an increase of 1 from Strike Dummy???.
- Seeker Strike contributes to the total damage of Perfected Strike.
=== Neutral ===
- If Hellraiser is in play, and Seeker Strike is drawn, Seeker Strike is automatically played against a random enemy.

### Obtaining Seeker Strike

Seeker Strike is a Colorless card, and therefore can be obtained in the following ways:
- Can be bought from the Merchant.
- Can be obtained from Monsters and Elites with Dingy Rug.
    - Can also appear in your starting Deck in the Daily Climb with the All Star modifier.
- Can be obtained from Lead Paperweight.
- Can be obtained from a card reward from the  option at Brain Leech.
- Can be obtained from the  option at Endless Conveyor if Fried Eel is offered.
