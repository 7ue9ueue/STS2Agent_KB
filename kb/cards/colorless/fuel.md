## Notes

### Interactions

=== Synergies ===
Because Fuel can only be created through Compact, any Defect cards that create Status cards inherently synergise.
- Boost Away: Adds one Dazed into your Discard Pile.
- Gunk Up: Adds one Slimed into your Discard Pile.
- TURBO: Adds one Void into your Discard pile.
- Fight Through: Adds two Wounds into your Discard pile
- Overclock: Adds one Burn into your Discard Pile.

Most Defect cards that synergise with generation of Status cards also synergise with Fuel.
- Iteration: Draw 2 the first time a Status card is drawn each turn.
    - This card is particularly synergistic with Compact because it helps you fill your Hand with multiple Statuses to Transform into Fuel all at once, and mitigates the threat of bad turns where your hand is full of Statuses with nothing good to play.
- Rocket Punch: Reduces its cost to 0  whenever a Status card is created for the turn.
- Smokestack: Deals 5 damage to all enemies whenever a Status card is created.
- Trash to Treasure: Channels one random Orb whenever a Status card is created.

=== Anti-Synergies ===
- Flak Cannon: Exhausts all Status cards in your deck, making them unable to be turned into Fuel by Compact.
- It is not recommended to remove Frantic Escape when fighting The Insatiable unless you can finish the fight soon.
