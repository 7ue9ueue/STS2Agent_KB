## Card Text

**Base**: Innate . Upgrade ALL your cards. Exhaust .



## Notes

### Interactions

=== Order ===
- Cards that are created _after_ Apotheosis has already been played (such as the Shivs created by Blade Dance, the random Common cards created by Hello World, or cards generated by Attack Potion, Skill Potion, or Power Potion) will not be upgraded.
- Playing Apotheosis while having Burning Sticks active will upgrade your cards before the copy of Apotheosis is created, so the copy will not be upgraded.
- Apotheosis does not upgrade itself.

=== Neutral ===
- Armaments and Drain Power may only be able to upgrade cards before Apotheosis is played.
    - If the player has cards or other effects that generate cards in the middle of combat, then Armaments and Drain Power will be able to upgrade them as normal since they will not have been upgraded by Apotheosis.

### Obtaining Apotheosis

Can be obtained by taking Jewelry Box from Nonupeipe's offering at the start of Act 3.

### Trivia

- "Apotheosis" is the act of becoming divine or god-like.
- The card art for Apotheosis is reminiscent of the art for the Priest talent of the same name in Blizzard Entertainment's _World of Warcraft_ and may have been the inspiration for its art in Slay the Spire (2).
    - The same art from World of Warcraft was re-used for the Hearthstone card of the same name, also developed by Blizzard Entertainment and set in the Warcraft universe.
