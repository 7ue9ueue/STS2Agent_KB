## Card Text

**Base**: Get farther away. Increase Sandpit by 1. Increase the cost of this card by 1.



## Notes

### Interactions

=== Synergies ===
As Frantic Escape is a Status card it interacts with the following:
- Compact will convert it into Fuel.
- Iteration will draw 2 cards when it is drawn if it is the first Status card drawn that turn.
- Flak Cannon will exhaust it to deal damage.

- **Cost increase is per-card permanent.** Each individual copy tracks its own cost — playing one copy doesn't bump the others. Track each copy's current cost separately when planning energy.
- Inserted into the deck by The Insatiable's Liquify Ground (3 to draw, 3 to discard, with 4 Sandpit). Sandpit decrements each enemy phase; play 1 FE/turn to maintain the counter. Missing 2 consecutive turns risks death once Sandpit ≤ 2.
- Play 1-cost copies early; saving them often turns them into 2-3 cost cards that compete with block and make Sandpit lethal.
