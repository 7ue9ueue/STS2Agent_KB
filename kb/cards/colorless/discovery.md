## Card Text

**Base**: Choose 1 of 3 random cards to add into your Hand. It's free to play this turn. Exhaust .

**Upgraded**: Choose 1 of 3 random cards to add into your Hand. It's free to play this turn.


## Notes

### Interactions

Discovery only produces cards associated with the character at hand, excluding Ancient cards.
*Neither Dingy Rug nor Prismatic Gem expands Discovery's potential card pool.
===Neutral===
*The following cards cannot be generated by Discovery:
    - Feed
    - Not Yet
    - The Hunt
    - The Scythe
    - Royalties
    - Genetic Algorithm

### Obtaining Discovery

Discovery is a Colorless card, and therefore can be obtained in the following ways:
- Can be bought from the Merchant.
- Can be obtained from Monsters and Elites with Dingy Rug.
    - Can also appear in your starting Deck in the Daily Climb with the All Star modifier.

- Can be obtained from Lead Paperweight.
- Can be obtained from a card reward from the  option at Brain Leech.
- Can be obtained from the  option at Endless Conveyor if Fried Eel is offered.
