## Card Text

**Base**: Choose an Attack or Power card. Add a copy of that card into your Hand.

**Upgraded**: Choose an Attack or Power card. Add 2 copies of that card into your Hand.

## Notes

### Interactions

=== Synergies ===
- Duplicating any card with "Strike" in its name using Dual Wield will increase the damage of Perfected Strike, as it counts all cards in your deck during combat, not your actual deck.

=== Neutral ===
- When a card is copied, any changes to that card are also copied. The duration of these changes is initially the same for any copy as for the original. Once created, all copies behave fully independently.
    - A changed energy cost is copied, through effects like Snecko Eye or Liquid Memories, or through the automatic energy change of cards like Stomp and Kingly Kick.
    - Changes to the values of the card are copied, such the increased damage of Rampage or Thrash.

- If a copy created by Dual Wield exceeds the hand size limit, it gets added to the discard pile.
