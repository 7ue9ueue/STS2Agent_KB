## Strategy Notes

<!-- Reserved for the meta-agent. Append lessons, combos, and heuristics here.
     Source facts live above the divider. -->
- Excellent early hallway burst from Arcane Scroll; can delete a low-HP attacker by exhausting starter filler cards.
- Track that it exhausts most or all of the hand for the combat, so use it to end or simplify fights rather than before needed block cards.
- Extra copies are more attractive with strong draw/energy support because one Fiend Fire can end a fight without needing to cycle back.
- High-priority target for Vigorous-style damage enchants because the bonus can apply across multiple exhausted-card damage packets.
- In live play under Spectral Knight Hex, Fiend Fire+ exhausted multiple Ethereal cards but only dealt one visible damage packet; verify lethal from the current damage preview before relying on hand size.
