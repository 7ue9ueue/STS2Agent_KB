## Strategy Notes

<!-- Reserved for the meta-agent. Append lessons, combos, and heuristics here.
     Wiki facts live above the divider. -->
- Body Slam+ is much stronger once the deck has repeatable large block sources such as Blood Wall, Dash, True Grit+, Taunt, and multiple Shrug It Offs.
- Treat Body Slam as a payoff, not a starter damage card; take it when the deck already has large block, Unmovable, Barricade, or enough draw to pair block and payoff consistently.
- Corruption+ improves Body Slam turns when the deck has several free block skills; convert block into damage before ending the turn.
- Unmovable+ and Anchor improve Body Slam+ by creating large block totals without extra setup energy; 0-cost damage is valuable on defensive setup turns.
- Barricade plus Unmovable turns retained block into a scaling Body Slam plan; bank block first, then convert it on Vulnerable or low-energy-tax turns.
