## Strategy Notes

<!-- Reserved for the meta-agent. Append lessons, combos, and heuristics here.
     Source facts live above the divider. -->
- Upgrade is a major breakpoint when boss setup cost matters; 1 energy is much easier to install before heavy turns.
- Pair with Feel No Pain and controlled exhaust to turn junk/status cards into draw plus block.
- Against Doormaker Hunger, played Attacks and Skills exhaust; Dark Embrace can turn that tax into draw, and with Anger can create a repeatable 0-cost attack loop if the live deck is thin enough.
- Do not rely on Dark Embrace draw during Doormaker Scrutiny because extra draw is blocked.
