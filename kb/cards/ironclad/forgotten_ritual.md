## Strategy Notes

<!-- Reserved for the meta-agent. Append lessons, combos, and heuristics here.
     Wiki facts live above the divider. -->
- With Corruption active, play any useful skill first so it exhausts, then Forgotten Ritual can be a free +3 Energy burst for Whirlwind or attack chains.
- Upgraded Ritual is takeable with several natural Exhaust cards such as Scrawl+, Infernal Blade+, True Grit+, and Fiend Fire+; treat it as conditional burst energy, not standalone ramp.
- Skill Potion Ritual can be excellent with a same-turn exhausting Skill such as Dominate+; it adds burst energy without consuming block cards the way Burning Pact or Fiend Fire lines can.
