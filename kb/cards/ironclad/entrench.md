## Card Text

**Base**: Double your Block .

## Notes

### Interactions

=== Order ===
- Intimidating Helmet will give its 4 Block before Entrench doubles Block, but Tuning Fork will give its 7 Block afterwards.
=== Synergies ===
==== Relics ====
- If Pael's Legion or Vambrace are active, Entrench will instead triple your Block.
    - If both are active, they will stack additively and Entrench will quintuple your Block.

=== Neutral ===
- As it doubles existing Block instead of giving Block directly, Entrench is unaffected by Frail, Dexterity or No Block from a previously played Panic Button.
