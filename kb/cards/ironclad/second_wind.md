## Strategy Notes

<!-- Reserved for the meta-agent. Append lessons, combos, and heuristics here.
     Source facts live above the divider. -->
- Scales as a defensive exhaust card, but play key Powers and draw Skills first because it exhausts all non-Attacks in hand.
- Very strong against status-heavy fights: converting Wounds/Burns/Dazed and other non-Attack junk into Block also shrinks the live deck for faster premium-card cycling.
- In long elite/boss fights, avoid exhausting too many Defends early unless the fight will end soon; over-thinning defensive cards can leave later attack turns with no block.
