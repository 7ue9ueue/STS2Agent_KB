## Card Text

**Base**: Can only be played if every card in your Hand is an Attack. Deal 14 damage.

**Upgraded**: Can only be played if every card in your Hand is an Attack. Deal 18 damage.

## Notes

### Interactions

=== Synergies ===
- Second Wind, and to a lesser degree Corruption and Ghost Seed can be used to fulfill the requirement to play this card.
- Synergizes well with hand-management through effects such as Exhaust or Discard.
- When drawing Clash through Unceasing Top, it will be the only card in hand, meaning it can be played.

=== Neutral ===
*Even if the condition is not met, Clash can still be played indirectly through effects such as Stampede and Distilled Chaos.

=== Anti-Synergies ===
- As opposed to most cards and strategies in this game, this card pairs poorly with effects that allow you to draw cards, except for previously mentioned Unceasing Top.
- It pairs poorly with Dark Embrace, Joss Paper, and Burning Sticks, since Exhausting non-Attack cards is a way to enable you to play Clash, but these can sometimes replace those cards with other non-Attack cards.
