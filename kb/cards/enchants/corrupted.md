# Corrupted

## Effect

Deal 50% more damage, but lose 2 HP.

## Sources And Targets

| Source | Source type | Target |
|---|---|---|
| Symbiote | Event: Hive, Glory | 1 card |

## Damage Ordering

Corrupted is a damage enchantment. Damage enchantments are applied to the card before Strength and before multiplicative effects such as Vulnerable or Weak.

## Notes

- Cards can only have one enchantment.
