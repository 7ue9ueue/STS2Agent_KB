# Enchants

Card enchantments are permanent positive modifiers added to individual cards in your Deck or to future card rewards. The wiki.gg Enchantments page says a card can only have one enchantment.

## Damage Enchant Heuristic

For attack-damage enchants, prefer attacks that deal damage multiple times or replay often. Each separate attack damage packet can receive the bonus, so X-cost repeat attacks and cards that hit once per condition usually outscale single-hit attacks.

High-value Ironclad targets include Whirlwind, Sword Boomerang, Tear Asunder, Spite, Twin Strike, Fight Me!, and Fiend Fire. Cross-class/colorless examples include Volley, Exterminate, and Peck.

The Strength wiki notes that enchantments such as Sharp, Vigorous, Momentum, and Favored are applied to the card before Strength; Weak's page also groups Sharp, Vigorous, and Momentum with other additive damage effects before multiplicative effects.
