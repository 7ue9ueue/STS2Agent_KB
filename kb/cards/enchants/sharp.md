# Sharp

## Effect

Increases damage on this card by X.

## Sources And Targets

| Source | Source type | Target |
|---|---|---|
| Self-Help Book | Event: All Acts | 1 Attack, Sharp 2 |
| Gnarled Hammer | Shop Relic | Up to 3 Attacks, Sharp 3 |

## Damage Ordering

Sharp is a damage enchantment. Damage enchantments are applied to the card before Strength and before multiplicative effects such as Vulnerable or Weak.

## Notes

- Cards can only have one enchantment.
