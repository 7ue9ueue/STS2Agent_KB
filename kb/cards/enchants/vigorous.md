# Vigorous

## Effect

The first time this card is played, it deals X additional damage.

## Sources And Targets

| Source | Source type | Target |
|---|---|---|
| Stone of All Time: Push | Event: Hive | 1 Attack, Vigorous 8, costs 6 HP |

## Damage Ordering

Vigorous is a damage enchantment. Damage enchantments are applied to the card before Strength and before multiplicative effects such as Vulnerable or Weak.

## Notes

- Cards can only have one enchantment.
