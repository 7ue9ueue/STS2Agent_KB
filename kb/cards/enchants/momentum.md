# Momentum

## Effect

Increase this card's attack damage by X this combat when played.

## Sources And Targets

| Source | Source type | Target |
|---|---|---|
| Punch Dagger | Shop Relic | 1 Attack, Momentum 5 |

## Damage Ordering

Momentum is a damage enchantment. Damage enchantments are applied to the card before Strength and before multiplicative effects such as Vulnerable or Weak.

## Notes

- Cards can only have one enchantment.

- Prefer repeat-hit, X-cost, replayed, or hand-returning Attacks; each separate attack damage packet can receive the increased damage, making Whirlwind, Sword Boomerang, Tear Asunder, Spite, Twin Strike, Fight Me!, Fiend Fire, Volley, Exterminate, and Peck stronger targets than most single-hit attacks.
