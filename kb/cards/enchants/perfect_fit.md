# Perfect Fit

## Effect

Whenever this would be shuffled into your Draw Pile, place it on the top instead.

## Sources And Targets

| Source | Source type | Target |
|---|---|---|
| Field of Man-Sized Holes | Event: Hive | 1 card |

## Notes

- Perfect Fit does not apply to the opening shuffle at combat start, so it is not the same as Innate.
- Cards can only have one enchantment.
