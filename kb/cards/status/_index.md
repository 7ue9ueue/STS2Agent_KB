## Strategy Notes

- Statuses are bad to draw, but exhausting them during combat is often good: it shrinks the live deck and helps cycle back to premium cards faster.
- Against status-clog enemies, cards like True Grit+, Burning Pact, Second Wind, and Fiend Fire turn status pollution into block, draw, damage, or consistency.
