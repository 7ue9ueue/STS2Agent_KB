## Card Text

**Base**: If this is in your Hand, it must be played before other cards. Eternal .



## Notes

### Interactions

=== Synergies ===
- Enthralled's effect can be bypassed in many ways:
    - Cards like Acrobatics can draw and then Discard it in one action.
    - Cards like Havoc, CHARGE!! and Cleanse can Exhaust or Transform it before drawing it.
    - Pael's Eye can Exhaust it without needing to play a card, before giving you an extra turn.
    - While Enthralled is in your Hand, other cards can still be played indirectly through means such as Decisions, Decisions, Stampede, and Distilled Chaos.

### Obtaining Enthralled

Enthralled can only be obtained by taking Blood-Soaked Rose from Vakuu's offering at the start of Act 3, which provides 1  at the start of each turn.
