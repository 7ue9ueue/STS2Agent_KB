## Notes

### Interactions

=== Synergies ===
As Folly is a Curse, Darkstone Periapt will give 6 Max HP when obtaining it.

As Folly is Innate, the downside of having it can be mitigated:
- Gambling Chip allows you to discard it away at the start of combat, meaning you won't draw it until after the first shuffle.
- Ring of the Snake, Ring of the Drake, and Bag of Preparation draw you additional cards on your first turn, lessening the impact of an Innate Curse in your starting hand.

=== Anti-Synergies ===
- Bellows will have one less card it can potentially Upgrade.
- As Clash can only be played when all cards in your hand are Attacks, if it is drawn at the same time as Folly, it cannot be played.

### Obtaining Folly

Folly can only be obtained by taking Preserved Fog from Vakuu's offering at the start of Act 3, which allows you to remove 5 cards from your Deck.

### Trivia

- The card art for Folly is a reference to the myth of Sisyphus.
    - The card art for Folly may also reference the image associated with the internet meme One Must Imagine Sisyphus Happy.
