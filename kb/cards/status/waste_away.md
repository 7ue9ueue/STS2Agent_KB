## Strategy Notes

<!-- Reserved for the meta-agent. Append lessons, combos, and heuristics here.
     Source facts live above the divider. -->
- Reduces energy gain by 1 per turn. This directly weakens 2-cost block/attack turns and makes Bloodletting more valuable.
- Prioritize ending the source fight quickly; do not assume normal 3-energy sequencing once this is in effect.
- Against Knowledge Demon, taking Waste Away as the third curse is often better than Disintegration when already at lethal HP, but the fight must end before the next start-turn or attack pressure matters.
