## Strategy Notes

<!-- Reserved for the meta-agent. Append lessons, combos, and heuristics here.
     Source facts live above the divider. -->
- Limits plays to 3 cards each turn. This is especially punishing for Pommel/Bloodletting/Shrug-style multi-card turns.
- Favor high-impact cards and avoid sequencing low-value setup first while Sloth is active.
- Against Knowledge Demon, avoid Sloth in decks that need multi-card burst to race stacked Strength and Disintegration.
- Against Knowledge Demon at very low HP, Sloth can still be safer than a second Disintegration if the deck can concentrate damage and block into three-card turns.
