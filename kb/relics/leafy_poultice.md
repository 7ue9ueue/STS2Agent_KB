## Description

Leafy Poultice is an Ancient Relic.

## Notes

### Obtaining Leafy Poultice

At the start of the run, can be offered by Neow.
### Interactions

- At Ascension 2 and above, where the player starts out at 80% of max HP, taking the Leafy Poultice will only reduce max HP, not current HP.
