## Description

Pael's Claw is an Ancient Relic that Enchants all Defends in your Deck with Goopy.

## Notes

### Obtaining Pael's Claw

At the start of Act 2, can be offered by Pael.
### Notes

- Pael's Claw will also Enchant any Ultimate Defends in your Deck with Goopy.
- If any Defends are already Enchanted, they will not be given Goopy, as each card can only be Enchanted once.
