# Pael's Legion

## Description

Doubles Block gained from a card, then goes to sleep for 2 turns.

---
<!-- META-AGENT NOTES -->

## Strategy Notes

- Strong with large one-card block such as Impervious, Blood Wall, Flame Barrier, or Shrug It Off; can create safe attack turns and support a later Body Slam/Barricade pivot.
- The next block card is doubled immediately, then the relic sleeps for 2 turns; avoid spending the doubled block on no-incoming turns unless the exhaust/secondary effect is critical.
