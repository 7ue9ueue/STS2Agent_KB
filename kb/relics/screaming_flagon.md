# Screaming Flagon

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Rarity | RARITY |
| Class | Any |

## Description

The Screaming Flagon is a Shop Relic in Slay the Spire 2. It deals 20 damage to all enemies at the end of each of your turns, but only if you have no cards in hand.

## Notes

### Notes

### Sources
- The Screaming Flagon can only be purchased from a Shop.

### Order
- The Screaming Flagon checks your hand before discarding your cards or exhausting Ethereal cards.
