## Description

Sea Glass is an Ancient Relic. Upon pickup, you see 15 cards from a class other than your own, and add any number of them to your deck.

The options always consist of 5 Commons, 5 Uncommons, and 5 Rares.

## Notes

### Obtaining Sea Glass

At the start of Act 2, can be offered by Orobas.
### Notes

This relic's name will be different depending on which class it offers cards from:
- Ironclad: Demon Glass
- Silent: Venom Glass
- Regent: Noble Glass
- Necrobinder: Lich Glass
- Defect: Gear Glass
