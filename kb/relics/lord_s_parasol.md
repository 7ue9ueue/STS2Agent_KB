# Lord's Parasol

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Rarity | Ancient |
| Class | Any |

## Description

Lord's Parasol is an Ancient Relic. Upon entering a shop after obtaining this relic you immediately obtain everything the Merchant sells at no cost. This includes cards, relics, potions, and the card removal service. You may not refuse anything obtained and cards will be sent straight into your deck. Potions will only be obtained if you have space for them, and if not they will remain available at their original price.

## Notes

### Sources

At the start of Act 3, Lord's Parasol can be offered by Vakuu.
### Interactions

### Order
Shop options are purchased left to right, top to bottom. Will happen upon entering a shop tile and not upon interacting with the Merchant, making it impossible to skip.

### Synergies
### Relics
- Molten Egg, Toxic Egg, and Frozen Egg: Cards of each relic's respective type will be pre-upgraded when added to the deck.
- The Courier: Lord's Parasol will purchase the entire initial shop inventory for free, and leave the player with a second re-stocked shop inventory that functions normally.
- Eternal Feather: Each shop visited will give the player seven new cards (minus 1 remove) to increment Eternal Feather.
- Fresnel Lens: Each added block card will gain the Nimble 2 enchantment.
- Lucky Fysh: Each shop visited will grant 105 Gold due to the seven cards added.
    - With Dragon Fruit this will also grant 7 Max HP per shop.
- Potion Belt and Alchemical Coffer: Grants space for the 3 Potions obtained for free each shop.

### Cards
- Perfected Strike, Crescent Spear, and Squeeze: Will increment their damage if any of their respective cards are added from the shop.
- Arsenal, and Heirloom Hammer: Can benefit from the 2 additional colourless cards added for free each shop, which are usually more difficult to obtain.

### Neutral
### Relics
- Bing Bong: Will forcibly add 2 copies of every card from the shop into your deck.

### Anti-Synergies
### Relics
- Membership Card, Amethyst Aubergine and Bowler Hat: As Lord's Parsol grants everything from the shop at no cost, the additional Gold these relics provide can only be spent on certain Events, and any potion that could not be purchased due to lack of room. With Membership Card specifically only usable with the latter.
    - With The Courier this will be mitigated completely and these relics will function normally on the restocked shop.
- Maw Bank: Will forcibly break upon entering any shop tile.
### Trivia

- A parasol (also known as an umbrella) is a type of personal implement most commonly used to protect against rain or to provide shade on sunny days.
    - The visual depiction of the Lord's Parasol seems to be a non-collapsable Umbrella that lacks a crook handle. In modern English vernacular a 'parasol' more commonly describes an older style of cloth umbrellas meant solely for providing shade, while a modern 'umbrella' typically connotes being made from a water-proof material for use in the rain.
