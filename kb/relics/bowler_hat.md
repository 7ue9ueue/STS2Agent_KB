## Description

Bowler Hat is a Common Relic which increases all Gold gains by 25%. It does not visually increase the amount gained by events or in combat rewards; the additional Gold is simply added to the current Gold total after the fact.

## Notes

### Obtaining Bowler Hat

- Can be dropped by fighting Elites, by buying from the Merchant or from Chests. See Chest and non-Chest chance at Relics.
- Can be obtained from the relics Calling Bell, Shovel and Black Star.

### Spawn Restrictions
### Interactions

- Because Bowler Hat's additional Gold is added _separate_ from the initial sum, it creates a second trigger for Dragon Fruit, increasing maximum HP by a total of 2 each time gold is collected.
