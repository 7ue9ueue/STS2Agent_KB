## Description

Golden Compass is an Ancient Relic offered by Tezcatara at the start of Act 2. It replaces the Act 2 map with a single path that is 2 floors longer than the regular Act 2 and always has the following Map Locations in order:
- Monster
- Unknown
- Monster
- Rest Site
- Monster
- Rest Site
- Unknown
- Treasure Room
- Unknown
- Treasure Room
- Unknown
- Merchant
- Elite
- Rest Site
- Elite
- Rest Site
- Boss Encounter

## Notes

### Obtaining Golden Compass

At the start of Act 2, it can be offered by Tezcatara.
### Trivia

The name of this relic might be a reference to the 1995 novel The Golden Compass.
