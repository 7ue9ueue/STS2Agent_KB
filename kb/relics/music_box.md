## Description

Music Box is an Ancient Relic. It creates an Ethereal copy of the first Attack you play each turn.

## Notes

### Obtaining Music Box

Can be offered at the start of Act 3 by Vakuu.
### Interactions

### Order
- The copy is created after the Attack resolves, meaning a copied Rampage will have its damage already increased and a copied Fiend Fire will not be immediately Exhausted.
