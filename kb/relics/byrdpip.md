## Description

Byrdpip is an Event Relic from the Byrdonis Nest event. Upon pickup, it gives the card Byrd Swoop, and a cosmetic Byrdpip pet will accompany you in battles.

## Notes

### Sources

- Can only be obtained by taking the Byrdonis Egg from the event Byrdonis Nest by selecting  then hatching it a Rest Site.
### Notes

- Removing the Byrd Swoop will not make the Byrdpip go away.
