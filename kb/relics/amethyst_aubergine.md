## Description

Amethyst Aubergine is a Common Relic.

## Notes

### Obtaining Amethyst Aubergine

- Can be dropped by fighting Elites, by buying from the Merchant or from Chests. See Chest and non-Chest chance at Relics.

- Can be obtained from the relics Calling Bell, Shovel and Black Star.
### Interactions

- The additional gold spawned by the relic is seperate from the gold dropped from defeating an enemy and thus triggers the Dragonfruit an additional time
