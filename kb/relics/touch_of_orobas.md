## Description

Touch of Orobas is an Ancient Relic. Upon pickup, it replaces your Starter Relic with an upgraded version.

## Notes

### Obtaining Touch of Orobas

At the start of Act 2, can be offered by Orobas.
### Notes

The upgraded Starter Relics you can obtain are as follows:
- **Ironclad**: Burning Blood -> Black Blood
- **Silent**: Ring of the Snake -> Ring of the Drake
- **Regent**: Divine Right -> Divine Destiny
- **Necrobinder**: Bound Phylactery -> Phylactery Unbound
- **Defect**: Cracked Core -> Infused Core
### Trivia

- Although the description in the in-game Compendium says it replaces your Starter Relic with an Ancient version, the rarities of the upgraded Relics are shown as Starter.
