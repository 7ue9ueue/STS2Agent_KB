## Description

Pael's Eye is an Ancient Relic. The first time each combat you end your turn without playing cards, it Exhausts your Hand and gives you an extra turn.

## Notes

### Obtaining Pael's Eye

Can be offered at the start of Act 2 by Pael.
### Interactions

### Order
- Pael's Eye will Exhaust your Hand before Stampede can play any Attacks.
    - However, Dark Embrace can draw Attacks in time for Stampede to play them.

### Synergies
- Any start of turn and end of turn effects, such as Mercury Hourglass and Consuming Shadow, will trigger on the turn you trigger Pael's Eye as well as the extra turn it gives you.
- Triggering Pael's Eye will always trigger Diamond Diadem as well, and the Diamond Diadem buff will persist until the next _enemy_ turn ends, meaning that you can halve the enemy's damage even after taking the extra turn from Pael's Eye with no restrictions.

### Neutral
- Triggering Pael's Eye will advance the turn counter, so it can allow you to trigger Relics like Horn Cleat and Chandelier earlier than would normally be possible. However, it can also waste their effects as Block and Energy do not usually persist between turns.

### Anti-Synergies
- Triggering Pael's Eye will Exhaust any Retained cards you may have wanted to keep.
