## Description

Seal of Gold is an Ancient Relic. After pickup, you will lose 5 Gold to gain  at the start of each turn.

## Notes

### Sources

Can be offered at the start of Act 2 by Tezcatara.
### Notes

- If you have less than 5 Gold, you will not gain  (or lose any Gold).
