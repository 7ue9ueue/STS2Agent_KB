# Cracked Core

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Rarity | Starter |
| Class | Any |

## Description

Cracked Core is the  Starter Relic for the Defect.

## Notes

### Sources

The Defect will always start every new run with this relic in its possession.
### Interactions

**Cracked Core** can be upgraded to Infused Core if the player obtains Touch of Orobas. This relic is randomly offered by the Ancient Orobas if they are encountered at the beginning of Hive.
### Synergies

### Order
- Symbiotic Virus will Channel 1 Dark after the 1 Lightning is Channeled.

### Synergies
- Cards and Relics that provide Focus, such as Data Disk, Hotfix, and Defragment, will increase the damage of the Lightning channeled by Cracked Core.
- Gold-Plated Cables, Tesla Coil, and Loop will allow the Lightning's passive to trigger multiple times in a turn.
- Metronome will get 1 stacks from Cracked Core Channeling 1 Lightning.
- Voltaic will be able to Channel 1 additional Lightning
- Dualcast, Quadcast, and Multi-Cast can be used to Evoke the Lightning.
- Thunder will deal additional damage to each enemy hit when the Lightning is Evoked.
- Coolant, Compile Driver, and Synchronize will increase their effectiveness for each Orb type Channeled.

### Anti-Synergies
- Hyperbeam will reduce the damage dealt by the Lightning.
### Trivia

- This relic is the same as the Defect's starting relic in Slay The Spire.
