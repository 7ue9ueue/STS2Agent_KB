## Description

Chemical X is a  Shop Relic in Slay the Spire 2.

## Notes

### Sources

Can only be bought from the Merchant. Only 1 Shop Relic is available per **Shop**, even with The Courier, since it cannot restock Shop Relics.
### Interactions

### Synergies
- Chemical X affects X-Cost cards even if they are played directly from the player's draw pile and no Energy is spent on them, such as from effects like sts2=true, or Distilled Chaos.
- While Chemical X is naturally useful with all X-Cost cards regardless of how much Energy is spent on them, it notably allows all X-Cost cards to be played with 0 Energy and still receive a significant benefit.

### Trivia

- Chemical X's name is a play-on-words on how it affects X-Cost cards in some way.
- Chemical X is a reference to the 1998 cartoon The Powerpuff Girls, where the titular girls were created in a lab using a substance called "Chemical X".
    - The flavor text is a direct reference to the series, featuring the iconic opening narration; _Sugar, spice, and everything nice. These were the ingredients chosen to create the perfect little girl. But Professor Utonium added an extra ingredient to the concoction... **Chemical X**! Thus, The Powerpuff Girls were born!_.
