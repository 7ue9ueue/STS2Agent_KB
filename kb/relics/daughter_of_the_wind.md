## Description

Daughter of the Wind is an Event Relic from the Doll Room event. Whenever you play an Attack, it will give 1 Block.

## Notes

### Sources

Can only be obtained from the Doll Room event.
### Interactions

### Order
- When attacking an enemy with Thorns, the Block will be gained after taking damage from Thorns.
