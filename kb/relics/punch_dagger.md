## Description

Punch Dagger is a  Shop Relic. Upon pickup, you Enchant an attack with Momentum 5, which increases its damage by 5 each time it is played this combat.

## Notes

### Sources

Can only be bought from the Merchant. Only 1 Shop Relic is available per **Shop**, even with The Courier, since it cannot restock Shop Relics.
### Interactions

### Synergies
- Multi-hit attacks, especially ones where the number of hits scales (such as Pull from Below, Barrage, and Radiate)
- Effects that return the card to hand or top of the deck (such as Thrumming Hatchet, Feral, and Nostalgia)
- Effects that replay the card (such as Echo Form and Hidden Gem)
