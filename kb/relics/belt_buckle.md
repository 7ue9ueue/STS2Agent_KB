## Notes

### Synergies
- The Delicate Frond will refill all potion slots every battle, meaning that you can play all potions at the start of each fight to activate the Belt Buckle without needing to worry about running out.

### Neutral
### Anti-Synergies
- Especially strong potions like the Ghost in a Jar, which you will often want to save for a particularly difficult combat rather than using right away, will prevent the Belt Buckle from activating until you use them.
