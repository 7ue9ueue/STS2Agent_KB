# Ice Cream

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Rarity | Unknown |
| Class | CHARACTERNAME |

## Description

Ice Cream is a  Rare Relic.   It prevents the player's Energy from being reset at the start of each turn, instead adding any remaining energy to the base value next turn. For example, if Ironclad has 3 base energy and uses 2, then at the start of the next turn he will have 4 Energy available.

## Notes

### Obtaining Ice Cream

- Can be dropped by fighting Elites, by buying from the Merchant or from Chests. See Chest and non-Chest chance at Relics.

- Can be obtained from the relics Calling Bell, Shovel and Black Star.
- Can be obtained from certain events.

- Can never be dropped by a **Small Chest**.
### Trivia

- Ice Cream depicts a 3-scoop cone of Neapolitan ice cream; a type of ice cream separated into 3 distinct sections of vanilla, chocolate, and strawberry ice cream flavors.
