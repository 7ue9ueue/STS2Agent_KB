# Infused Core

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Rarity | Unknown |
| Class | Any |

## Description

3=2 is the Starter Upgrade of the Defect's 3=2. It Channels 3 Lightning at the start of combat instead of 1.

## Notes

### Sources

Can be obtained by taking the Touch of Orobas from Orobas. Replaces 3=2.
### Interactions

### Order
- Symbiotic Virus will Channel 1 Dark after the 3 Lightning is Channeled.

### Synergies
- Cards and Relics that provide Focus, such as Data Disk, Hotfix, and Defragment, will increase the damage of the Lightning channeled by Infused Core.
- Gold-Plated Cables, Tesla Coil, and Loop will allow the Lightning's passive to trigger multiple times in a turn.
- Metronome will get 3 stacks from Infused Core Channeling 3 Lightning.
- Voltaic will be able to Channel 3 additional Lightning
- Dualcast, Quadcast, and Multi-Cast can be used to Evoke the Lightning.
- Thunder will deal additional damage to each enemy hit when the Lightning is Evoked.
- Coolant, Compile Driver, and Synchronize will increase their effectiveness for each Orb type Channeled.

### Anti-Synergies
- Hyperbeam will reduce the damage dealt by the Lightning.
