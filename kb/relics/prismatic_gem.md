## Description

Prismatic Gem is an Ancient Relic. After you obtain this Relic, your card rewards will show cards from other colors excluding Colorless Cards. Additionally, you gain  at the start of each turn.

## Notes

### Sources

After fighting a Boss in Act 1, it can be offered by Orobas.
### Notes

When playing as any character except the Defect, you gain 1 Orb Slot when you first play an Orb card during combat
### Interactions

Sources of cards that are not card rewards, such as White Noise or potions which offer a choice of cards when used, will still only grant cards from your current character.

### Neutral
#### Relics
Dingy Rug: Colorless Cards also appear in card rewards
### Trivia

- The effect of this relic is similar to Prismatic Shard from the first Slay the Spire, however Colorless Cards will not appear and an extra Energy is given at the start of your turn.
