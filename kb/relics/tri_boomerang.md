## Description

Tri-Boomerang is an Ancient Relic.

## Notes

### Obtaining Tri-Boomerang

At the start of Act 3, can be offered by Tanx.
### Interactions

The "Instinct" enchantment from Tanx's Tri-Boomerang will double Body Slam's block-derived damage.
