## Description

Scroll Boxes is an Ancient Relic. Upon pickup, you lose all Gold and choose 1 of 2 packs of 3 cards to add to your Deck.

Each pack contains 1 random Uncommon and 2 random Commons. You must take one of the packs before proceeding.

There is a 1% chance when playing Defect that one of the card packs is 3 copies of Claw.

## Notes

### Obtaining Scroll Boxes

At the start of the run, can be offered by Neow.
