## Description

Pendulum is a Common Relic.

## Notes

### Sources

- Can be dropped by fighting Elites, by buying from the Merchant or from Chests. See Chest and non-Chest chance at Relics.

- Can be obtained from the relics Calling Bell, Shovel and Black Star.
### Interactions

### Order
- The draw will resolve immediately when shuffling your Draw Pile.
    - For example, when playing Acrobatics or Decisions, Decisions and drawing more cards than are in your Draw Pile, Pendulum's draw will occur before you choose a card to discard for Acrobatics or a Skill to play for Decisions, Decisions. It will also draw before Havoc plays the top card of your Draw Pile.
