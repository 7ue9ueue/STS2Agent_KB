# Meat Cleaver

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Rarity | Ancient |
| Class | Any |

## Description

Meat Cleaver is an  Ancient Relic.  This relic gives the Player the option to remove 2 cards from their Deck and gain 9 Max HP at Rest Sites. This relic is only obtainable at the start of Act 3.

## Notes

### Notes

At the start of Act 3, if the Ancient is Tanx, he has a random chance to offer this relic. This is the only way to acquire it.
### Interactions

If the player's deck does not include at least 2 removable cards, Cook cannot be chosen.

### Synergies
- Miniature Tent has powerful synergy with any relic that provides additional Rest Site options. When the Player has multiple such relics (eg. Girya or Shovel), Miniature Tent improves further.
- Relics that improve Rest Sites regardless of action (eg. Eternal Feather or Venerable Tea Set) may make Meat Cleaver more appealing.

### Anti-Synergies
- Other relics that provide additional options at Rest Sites (eg. Girya or Shovel) tend to lose some value since only one option may be chosen.
- Forbidden Grimoire can make the Meat Cleaver's card removal redundant.
