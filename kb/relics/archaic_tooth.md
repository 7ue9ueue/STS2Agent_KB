## Description

Archaic Tooth is an Ancient Relic that replaces one of your Starter Cards (not including Strikes or Defends) with an upgraded Ancient version.

## Notes

### Obtaining Archaic Tooth

At the start of Act 2, can be offered by Orobas.
### Notes

The transformed cards you can obtain are as follows:
- **Ironclad**: Bash -> Break
- **Silent**: Neutralize -> Suppress
- **Regent**: Falling Star -> Meteor Shower
- **Necrobinder**: Unleash -> Protector
- **Defect**: Dualcast -> Quadcast

- If the original card was Upgraded and/or Enchanted, the resulting Ancient card will also be Upgraded and/or Enchanted.
### Trivia

- If you have the egg for the respective card (Molten Egg for Attacks, Toxic Egg for Skills) before transforming the card, after taking Archaic Tooth the Transformed card will then be Upgraded.
