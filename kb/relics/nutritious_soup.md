## Description

Nutritious Soup is an Ancient Relic that Enchants all Strikes in your Deck with Tezcatara's Ember.

## Notes

### Obtaining Nutritious Soup

At the start of Act 2, can be offered by Tezcatara.
### Notes

- Nutritious Soup will _not_ Enchant any Ultimate Strikes in your Deck.
- If any Strikes are already Enchanted, they will not be given Tezcatara's Ember, as each card can only be Enchanted once.
