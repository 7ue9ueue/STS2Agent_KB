## Description

Glass Eye is an Ancient Relic. Upon pickup, you are offered 5 card rewards, with their rarities fixed at 2 sets of 3 Commons, 2 sets of Uncommons, and 1 set of Rares.

At the start of Act 2, can be offered by Orobas.
