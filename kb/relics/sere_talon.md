## Description

Sere Talon is an Ancient Relic. It adds 3 copies of Wish to the player's deck as well as 2 random Curses. The Curses added will never include Ascender's Bane, Bad Luck, Curse of the Bell, Enthralled, Folly, Greed, Poor Sleep, or Spore Mind.

## Notes

### Sources

After fighting a Boss in Act 2, it can be offered by Vakuu.
