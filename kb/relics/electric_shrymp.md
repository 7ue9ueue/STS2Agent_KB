## Description

Electric Shrymp is an Ancient Relic. Upon pickup, you choose a Skill to Enchant with Imbued, making it automatically play itself for free at the start of each combat. X-cost cards such as Cascade take into account the current value of X (influenced by Chemical X) but do not consume any energy.

After being played, the card will go into your Discard Pile, or Exhaust if it has Exhaust.

## Notes

### Obtaining Electric Shrymp

At the start of Act 2, can be offered by Orobas.
### Interactions

### Order
- The Imbued Skill will be played after other start-of-combat effects occur such as Toolbox and Anchor, and after drawing cards on your first turn.
    - The Imbued Skill is forced to start combat at the bottom of your Draw Pile, so you cannot usually 'waste' a draw on it (unless you draw your whole Draw Pile).

### Synergies
- Strong but expensive skills like Royal Gamble, Nightmare or Decisions, Decisions benefit significantly from being played instantly for free.
