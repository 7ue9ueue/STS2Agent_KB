## Description

Looming Fruit is an Ancient Relic.

## Notes

### Obtaining Looming Fruit

At the start of Act 3, can be offered by Nonupeipe.
### Trivia

- The amount of max HP gained is equivalent to the Strawberry, Pear, and Mango combined.
- Looming Fruit's name is a reference to the clothing company Fruit of the Loom.
- The sprite used for Looming Fruit has a 50% chance to either include or not include a cornucopia. This will always stay the same on a given profile.
    - This is a reference to the Fruit of the Loom logo, which depicts a pile of fruit but is often mistakenly believed to also include a cornucopia.
