## Description

Pael's Growth is an Ancient Relic. Upon pickup, you Enchant a card with Clone, allowing you to duplicate _every_ copy of it at Rest Sites.

## Notes

### Sources

Can be offered at the start of Act 2 by Pael.
### Notes

- Cloning at a Rest Site will duplicate every card with Clone at once.
- The copies will also have Clone, meaning that each time you use Clone, the number of copies in your deck doubles.
