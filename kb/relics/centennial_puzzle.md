## Description

Centennial Puzzle is a Common Relic.

## Notes

### Sources

- Can be dropped by fighting Elites, by buying from the Merchant or from Chests. See Chest and non-Chest chance at Relics.
- Can be obtained from the relics Calling Bell, Shovel and Black Star.
### Interactions

- Centennial Puzzle only triggers when _HP is lost_, not when _taking damage_. Receiving damage that is fully Blocked will not trigger the relic.
    - Effects that cause the player to _lose HP_, rather than take damage, will circumvent any Block present.
### Synergies
#### Cards
- Centennial Puzzle can be triggered by any cards that invoke HP loss during the player's turn, such as Bloodletting and Brand.
    - Because Brightest Flame deducts from the player's _maximum_ HP, rather than their _current_ HP, it will only trigger Centennial Puzzle if the player's current HP is at its maximum. Conversely, if they are below maximum HP (IE they have lost HP at some point prior), their maximum HP will be reduced but Centennial Puzzle will _not_ trigger.
#### Relics
Royal Poison's HP loss triggers Centennial Puzzle.
### Neutral
- Centennial Puzzle can be triggered by attacking into an enemy with Thorns.
- Tungsten Rod can potentially negate HP loss that would otherwise trigger Centennial Puzzle.
    - Because Demon Tongue _heals_ HP rather than prevents HP loss, it does _not_ negate Centennial Puzzle's damage trigger.
### Anti-Synergies
- End-of-turn damage effects (Such as Beckon or Regret) will trigger Centennial Puzzle and immediately discard its drawn cards with the rest of the hand (provided effects such as Retain and Runic Pyramid are not in play).
